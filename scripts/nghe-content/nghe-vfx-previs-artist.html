Trước khi quay scene Hulk smash trong "Avengers", scene dragon battle trong "Game of Thrones", scene chase trong "Top Gun: Maverick" — Director + DP + VFX team đã thấy entire scene as 3D animation. Đó là tác phẩm của Previs Artist.

Previs (Pre-visualization) Artist VFX là chuyên gia tạo 3D animated rough version của VFX-heavy scene trước khi quay. Giúp creative team align trên camera, blocking, choreography, timing. Là foundation tool của modern VFX-heavy production — từ Pirates of Caribbean trở đi, mọi blockbuster đều previs heavy.

01Previs Artist VFX là ai?

Previs Artist là chuyên gia tạo previsualization 3D — animated rough cho VFX-heavy scene. Phân loại theo phase: (1) Previs (pre-visualization) — early stage, before shoot, rough creative direction; (2) Techvis (technical visualization) — pre-shoot, technical detail (crane reach, camera limit, stunt safety); (3) Postvis — during post, integrate temp VFX với live action plate cho edit + screening. Phân loại theo studio: Specialized Previs studio (The Third Floor, Day For Nite, Halon Entertainment, Proof Inc) — leader globally; VFX studio Previs department (ILM, Weta); In-house production company. Phân loại theo project: Action / blockbuster, Marvel / superhero, Sci-fi + fantasy, Animated feature.

Khác với Storyboard Artist (2D panel) — Previs 3D animated cinematic. Khác với Layout Artist (animation) — Previs for live action production; Layout for animation. Khác với Animator — Previs rough quick; Animator polished final. Khác với VFX Artist post — Previs pre-shoot conceptual. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, VFX Supervisor, DP / Cinematographer, Stunt Coordinator, Production Designer, 1st AD, Editor (postvis).

  • Block VFX scene as 3D animation
  • Camera + lens match production reality
  • Iterate với Director vision
  • Handoff data cho production + post

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Previs Artist work tại previs studio specialized (Third Floor, Halon) hoặc VFX studio (ILM, Weta in-house). Báo cáo Previs Supervisor / VFX Supervisor / Director. Workflow per sequence: receive script + storyboard → asset prep (character placeholder, environment rough) → block 3D scene (1-2 tuần per sequence) → review với Director → iterate → final previs sequence. Phase: previs (pre-production, 3-9 tháng), techvis (immediately pre-shoot, 1-3 tuần), postvis (post-production, ongoing).

02Công việc của một Previs Artist VFX

1. Receive script + storyboard + reference

Receive script + storyboard from Director. Study reference: visual mood board, similar scene from past film, environment, character design. Meet Director + VFX Supervisor + DP. Hiểu vision: scene mood, character action, key beat, camera intent. Take note + sketch reference.

03Previs Artist VFX cần giỏi gì?

🛠️ Maya / Blender mastery
🎬 Cinematography + camera
🎭 Animation + acting
📖 Visual storytelling
⚡ Speed + iteration
💬 Director collaboration
Maya / Blender mastery

Maya primary tool. Master rigging basic, animation, camera, scene assembly. Blender growing in indie + open source. Unreal Engine 5 for virtual production / realtime previs (Mandalorian StageCraft style — modern trend).

Cinematography + camera

Hiểu camera language: focal length effect (wide vs tele), camera move (dolly, crane, handheld, Steadicam), lens characteristic, shot type (CU, MS, WS). Study cinematography: "The Five C's of Cinematography" (Mascelli), "Cinematography" (Brown). Watch films analytically.

Animation + acting

Rough but readable animation. Hiểu Disney 12 principle basic. Acting fundamental — character body language convey emotion + intent. Foundation animator skill.

Visual storytelling

Foundation. Sequence shot together tells story. Hiểu beat, pacing, emotional arc. Study film + storyboard + comic. Read: "In the Blink of an Eye" (Murch), "The Visual Story" (Block).

Speed + iteration

Previs designed for fast iteration. Build personal template + asset library. Quick blockout. Hit deadline. Speed > polish — that's the point of previs.

Director collaboration

Work with Director directly. Listen vision. Translate to 3D. Suggest creative option. Soft skill important — Director famous + opinionated. Diplomatic + responsive.

Background: Animation / VFX / Film Production / Cinematography. VN: FPT Arena, MAAC, SKDA Hà Nội, Arena Multimedia, RMIT. Quốc tế: Gnomon School, SCAD Animation + VFX, Vancouver Film School Animation, Sheridan College Animation, Ringling. Specialty course: The Third Floor + Halon offer mentorship + internship, FXPHD previs course.

2
Junior Previs Artist 2–4 năm

Specialized previs studio: The Third Floor (top globally, LA + London), Halon Entertainment (Burbank), Day For Nite, Proof Inc. VFX studio in-house previs: ILM, Weta, DNEG. Apply with strong reel (3D animation + camera work). Hire fewer entry-level — typically transition from Animator.

3
Mid Previs Artist 4–7 năm

Lead sequence work. Direct collaboration với Director. Build relationship cho future show.

4
Senior Previs Artist / Previs Supervisor 7–12 năm

Lead previs department on show. Manage team. Liaise với Director + VFX Supervisor.

5
Previs Director 12+ năm

Top role. Direct entire previs vision on major project. Brand reputation.

6
Sự nghiệp Previs Artist VFX

Long-term path: (1) Previs Director / Supervisor at top studio; (2) 2nd Unit Director — direct VFX-heavy scene on set; (3) Director transition — apply visual sense (rare but happens); (4) VFX Supervisor; (5) Animator transition — full polish animation; (6) Virtual production specialist — Unreal Engine, StageCraft. Lương: VN Previs Artist mid 30-65M/tháng (rare in VN — most work international); senior 65M-150M+. International: $80K-$130K mid; $130K-$220K senior; Supervisor $220K-$380K+.
