01Lookbook Designer là ai?

Lookbook Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế cuốn lookbook + visual content cho fashion brand. Phân loại theo brand: (1) Luxury Fashion Lookbook — Gucci, Dior, Saint Laurent — high-art, conceptual; (2) Designer brand Lookbook — independent designer, contemporary; (3) Mass market Lookbook — Zara, H&M, Uniqlo — commercial, accessible; (4) Streetwear Lookbook — Supreme, Off-White — youth culture; (5) VN brand Lookbook — boutique designer (Thuy Design House, Cong Tri, Hà Trí Hiếu), retail (Routine, Maison Online). Phân loại theo medium: Print lookbook (premium, gift to press / VIP), Digital lookbook (web + social + email), Video lookbook (motion).

Khác với Fashion Stylist (style on model) — Lookbook Designer take styled photo + design layout. Khác với Fashion Photographer (shoot photo) — Lookbook designer curate + design with photo. Khác với Graphic Designer general — Lookbook Designer fashion-specific. Khác với Magazine Editor — adjacent skill but Lookbook brand-side, magazine editorial-side. Hợp tác chặt với: Fashion Designer / Creative Director, Photographer, Stylist, Model + Casting, Hair + Makeup, Production Manager, Printer + Production house.

Fashion sense + brand awareness

Foundation. Live + breath fashion. Follow weekly: Vogue, Business of Fashion, Highsnobiety, Hypebeast, SHOWstudio. Study seminal lookbook: Margiela, Comme des Garçons, Helmut Lang, Phoebe Philo's Céline. Understand brand identity + heritage of multiple house.

Editorial layout + typography

Editorial design fundamental. Study magazine layout (i-D, Dazed, AnOther, Self Service, Purple, Apartamento). Hiểu grid, baseline, hierarchy, white space, typographic detail. Read: "Editorial Design" (Cath Caldwell + Yolanda Zappaterra).

Photography curation + retouching

Eye for great fashion photo. Hiểu lighting style (high-fashion editorial dramatic vs commercial clean). Basic Photoshop retouching: skin smoothing (don't overdo), color grading, dust removal. Color management for print (CMYK, ICC profile).

Tool: InDesign, Photoshop, Figma, Premiere

Adobe InDesign — primary tool for print lookbook (industry standard). Photoshop — retouching + image prep. Illustrator for vector. Figma — digital lookbook + web mockup. Premiere + After Effects for video lookbook. Lightroom for photo management.

Print production knowledge

Hiểu paper weight + finish (matte / glossy / uncoated / silk), binding type, special finish (foil, emboss, deboss, spot UV, die-cut). Print process: offset, digital, screen. Press check at printer. VN printer: In Ấn Anh Quân, Sao Việt, Phuongnam Printing. Premium international: Optimum, Conveyor.

Creative direction + collaboration

Work với photographer, stylist, model, designer. Strong communication. Defend creative decision. Open to feedback. Time + budget management.
