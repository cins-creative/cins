Tại Pixar, Disney, DreamWorks — trước khi animator polish character actions trong "Toy Story 5" hoặc "Frozen 3", sequence đã được rough-blocked bằng 3D animation. Đó là tác phẩm của Previs Artist hoạt hình.

Previs Artist hoạt hình là chuyên gia tạo previsualization 3D rough cho sequence animation. Test camera, blocking, timing, pacing trước Animation full production. Critical step trong animation pipeline cho Director vision concrete trước commit resource. Trong animation pipeline thường gọi là Layout / Rough Layout / Stereoscopic Layout — Previs cũng phổ biến.

01Previs Artist hoạt hình là ai?

Previs Artist hoạt hình là chuyên gia rough block 3D sequence cho animation. Phân loại theo phase: (1) Story Previs / Animatic 3D — early, after storyboard, before layout; (2) Rough Layout / Previs — establish camera + blocking with rough rigged character; (3) Final Layout — refined, ready cho Animator. Phân loại theo studio: Major animation (Pixar, Disney, DreamWorks, Sony Pictures Animation, Illumination) — large team; Mid + indie (Sparx*, Sun Wolf, VinTaTa) — smaller team multi-hat; Anime studio typically less previs-heavy (more direct from storyboard to animation). Phân loại theo project: Feature animation, TV series, Short film.

Khác với Storyboard Artist (2D panel) — Previs 3D animated. Khác với Animator — Previs rough quick; Animator polished final. Khác với Layout Artist — overlap heavy; sometime synonymous, sometime sequence (Previs first, Layout refine). Khác với Director — Previs execute Director vision. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, Head of Story / Story Artist, Editor, Animator, Cinematographer (animation), Producer.

  • Block sequence as 3D animation
  • Camera + lens choice cho cinematic
  • Iterate với Director vision
  • Handoff cho Animation full production

Previs Artist hoạt hình work tại animation studio. Báo cáo Layout / Previs Supervisor / Director. Feature animation total: 6-12 tháng previs phase.

02Công việc của một Previs Artist hoạt hình

1. Receive storyboard + animatic + asset

Receive storyboard + animatic (2D timing version) from Story team. Get asset: rough rigged character (basic skeleton), rough environment, prop. Meet Director + Head of Layout. Hiểu sequence intent: emotional beat, key action, camera direction.

03Previs Artist hoạt hình cần giỏi gì?

🛠️ Maya / Blender mastery
🎬 Cinematography (animation context)
🎭 Animation principle + acting
📖 Visual storytelling
⚡ Speed + iteration
💬 Director + Animator collaboration
Maya / Blender mastery

Maya primary animation industry tool. Master rigging basic, camera, scene assembly, animation block. Blender growing alternative. Some studio use proprietary tool (Pixar Presto). Unreal Engine for realtime previs growing in animation too.

Cinematography (animation context)

Animation cinematography rich — camera can do anything (no physical limit). Hiểu lens language: anamorphic vs spherical, focal length effect. Study live action cinematography. Read: "The Five C's" (Mascelli). Watch Pixar / Disney / Studio Ghibli analytically.

Animation principle + acting

Foundation. Disney 12 principle. Acting fundamental — body language convey emotion. Even rough previs need acting choice readable. Practice life drawing + sketch acting reference.

Visual storytelling

Sequence shot together = story. Beat, pacing, emotional arc. Study film + animatic. Read: "In the Blink of an Eye" (Murch), "The Visual Story" (Block), "Directing the Story" (Glebas).

Speed + iteration

Previs designed for fast iteration — that's the point. Build template + workflow. Quick blockout. Multiple option per session.

Director + Animator collaboration

Work với Director directly. Listen + translate vision to 3D. Hand off to Animator with clear intent — Animator build on Previs foundation. Soft skill important.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Previs Artist hoạt hình?

1
Học Animation / 3D 3–4 năm

Background: Animation / 3D Animation / Film. VN: FPT Arena, MAAC, SKDA Hà Nội, Arena Multimedia, RMIT. Quốc tế: Gnomon School, SCAD Animation, CalArts Animation, Sheridan College, Vancouver Film School, Ringling. Specialty: Animation Mentor + Animation Collaborative online.

2
Junior Previs / Layout Artist 2–4 năm

VN animation studio: Sun Wolf, VinTaTa, Sparx*, Sconnect, Alpha Animation, Cyclo Animation. International: Pixar, Disney, DreamWorks, Sony Pictures Animation, Illumination, Aardman, Studio Ghibli. Apply with strong layout / camera reel.

3
Mid Previs / Layout Artist 4–7 năm

Lead sequence work. Direct collaboration với Director. Develop signature camera sense.

4
Senior + Layout Supervisor 7–12 năm

Lead Layout / Previs department on project. Manage team. Establish camera language for film.

5
Cinematographer Animation / Director of Photography animation 12+ năm

Top role — full DP-level responsibility on feature animation. Strategic camera direction throughout film.

6
Sự nghiệp Previs Artist hoạt hình

Long-term path: (1) Cinematographer Animation / DP — top creative role; (2) Layout Supervisor / Head of Layout; (3) Director transition — apply visual sense; (4) Animator transition — full polish work; (5) VFX Previs transition — live action; (6) Educator. Lương: VN Previs / Layout Artist junior 12-25M/tháng; mid 25-55M; senior 55M-130M+; Layout Supervisor / DP 130M-280M+. International: $65K-$100K mid; $100K-$170K senior; Supervisor $170K-$300K+; DP Animation $250K-$450K (Pixar / Disney level). Previs / Layout strong career path animation — creative + technical balance + clear progression.
