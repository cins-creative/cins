01Webtoon Artist là ai?

Webtoon Artist là chuyên gia mobile-first vertical comic creator. Phân loại theo role: (1) Solo Webtoon Artist — viết + vẽ + ink + color (full creator); (2) Studio Webtoon Artist — vai trò trong studio (sketcher, inker, colorist); (3) Writer-only — work với partner artist; (4) Cover / illustration specialist; (5) Storyboard + breakdown specialist. Phân loại theo genre: (1) Romance — popular, 30%+ market; (2) Action / fantasy — Solo Leveling, Tower of God; (3) Horror / thriller; (4) Slice of life + comedy; (5) BL / GL — niche but loyal; (6) Sport / school. Phân loại theo platform: (1) Naver Webtoon (LINE Webtoon) — global biggest; (2) Kakao Page + KakaoTalk; (3) Tappytoon; (4) Lezhin; (5) Comicola Vietnam; (6) Tappy Vietnam; (7) VinaToon, ShareToon (VN). Phân loại theo career: (1) Aspiring / amateur; (2) Published Webtoon Artist; (3) Hit-series Artist; (4) Studio Founder.

Khác với Manga Artist — Manga horizontal print B&W traditional, Webtoon vertical mobile color episodic. Khác với Comic Artist (Western) — Western page-based traditional, Webtoon scroll-based vertical. Khác với Illustrator — Illustrator single image, Webtoon sequential narrative. Khác với Animator — Animator moving image, Webtoon still image sequential. Hợp tác chặt với: Writer / Scriptwriter (if collab), Editor (platform), Colorist + Inker (if studio), Translator (for global), Platform PD (Product Manager), Fanbase + reader (engagement).

03Webtoon Artist cần giỏi gì?

💻 Clip Studio + Procreate

Drawing + anatomy mastery

Figure drawing. Face. Expression. Hand. Read: "Drawing the Head and Hands" (Loomis), "Figure Drawing for All It's Worth" (Loomis), "Force: Dynamic Life Drawing" (Mattesi).

Vertical scroll layout

Long vertical pacing. Cliffhanger + scroll reveal. Whitespace beats. Different from page comic. Read: "Webtoon Master Guide" (LINE Webtoon Naver), study Solo Leveling + Tower of God layout.

Color + lighting

Mood per scene. Time of day. Emotion via color. Read: "Color and Light" (Gurney), "Imaginative Realism" (Gurney).

Storytelling + pacing

Hook every episode. Cliffhanger. Character arc. Read: "Story" (McKee), "Save the Cat!" (Snyder), "Understanding Comics" (McCloud).

Clip Studio + Procreate

Clip Studio Paint industry standard cho comic + webtoon. Procreate iPad popular. Photoshop finishing. 3D model assist (Sketchup, Blender) cho background.

Speed + stamina

50-80 panel per tuần brutal pace. Burnout common. Workflow optimize. Studio team essential at scale. Health management.
