User Researcher là chuyên gia tìm hiểu nhu cầu, hành vi, pain point của user qua interview, survey, usability test, ethnographic study, diary study. Cung cấp insight làm input cho design decision. Critical cho product không guess + grounded in user reality. Top tech (Google, Meta, Airbnb) đều invest mạnh vào User Research function.

01User Researcher là ai?

User Researcher (UXR) là chuyên gia research user behavior + need. Phân loại theo method: (1) Qualitative researcher — interview, observation, diary study (depth, why); (2) Quantitative researcher — survey, analytics, A/B test (breadth, what); (3) Mixed-method — both. Phân loại theo phase: (1) Generative research — discover problem trước design (foundational); (2) Evaluative research — test design + measure usability; (3) Strategic research — market + persona long-term. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Product researcher; (2) Service designer-researcher — broader service touchpoint; (3) Design strategist — strategy + business; (4) Field researcher — emerging market, ethnographic. Phân loại theo career: (1) Junior UXR; (2) UXR; (3) Senior UXR; (4) Lead / Principal UXR; (5) Director of Research. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Tech in-house; (2) Research consultancy — Userhabits, NielsenIQ, Decision Lab; (3) Design agency; (4) Freelance.

Khác với Market Research — Market broader (sales + brand + competitor), UXR focus user behavior with product. Khác với Data Analyst — Data quant only, UXR qual + quant. Khác với UX Designer — UX Designer design, UXR research + inform design. Một số UX Designer làm research light, but dedicated UXR deeper. Hợp tác chặt với: UX Designer + Product Designer, Product Manager, Data Analyst, Engineer (technical feasibility), Marketing (persona, segment), Customer Support (feedback channel).

02Công việc của một User Researcher

1. Research question + study design

Foundation. Stakeholder interview: PM + Designer + Engineer — what they need to know. Research question define: clear, answerable. Method selection: interview, survey, usability test, diary, ethnographic — match question. Sample design: target user, sample size (5-8 for usability, 20-50 for interview, 200+ for survey). Discussion guide / survey draft. Pilot test: 1-2 participant test guide. Recruit plan: internal panel, external agency.

03User Researcher cần giỏi gì?

👂 Listening + interview
📊 Research method literacy
🔍 Synthesis + pattern
📝 Writing + storytelling
💬 Stakeholder + influence
🧠 Curiosity + empathy
Listening + interview

Active listening. Probe (5 why). Non-leading question. Hold judgment. Read: "Just Enough Research" (Hall), "Interviewing Users" (Portigal), "The Mom Test" (Fitzpatrick).

Research method literacy

Qualitative + quantitative method. Statistics basic (mean, median, significance). Sampling. Bias awareness. Read: "Quantifying the User Experience" (Sauro + Lewis), "Observing the User Experience" (Kuniavsky).

Synthesis + pattern

Connect dot across participant. Theme distill. Insight crystallize. Tools: Dovetail, Reframer, Miro, FigJam. Read: "Universal Methods of Design" (Martin + Hanington).

Writing + storytelling

Report writing concise + compelling. Narrative arc. Quote use effectively. Visual + data combo. Read: "Storytelling with Data" (Knaflic), "Made to Stick" (Heath).

Stakeholder + influence

Present + push back tactfully. Sell insight to busy PM + leadership. Build research culture. Long-term relationship. Read: "Articulating Design Decisions" (Greever).

Curiosity + empathy

Genuine curious about user life. Empathic. Non-judgmental. Cultural sensitivity. Patient — research take time. Anthropological mindset.
