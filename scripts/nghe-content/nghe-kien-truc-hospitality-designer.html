Khi bạn check-in Park Hyatt Tokyo và lobby cảm giác như một bức tranh; khi Six Senses Ninh Bình villa hòa quyện thiên nhiên đến mức bạn quên đang trong resort; khi Capella Sài Gòn chợt làm bạn thấy mình đang ở Indochina 1920s — bạn đang trải nghiệm tác phẩm của Hospitality Designer.

Hospitality Designer thiết kế khách sạn, resort, nhà hàng, spa, bar, club — biến không gian thành experience đầy đủ. Là segment lớn nhất + complex nhất của ngành interior design, cần hiểu cả branding, F&B operation, guest journey, sustainability.

01Hospitality Designer là ai?

Hospitality Designer (Nhà thiết kế khách sạn & nhà hàng) là chuyên gia thiết kế interior + experience cho không gian phục vụ. Phân loại theo property type: (1) Hotel — boutique 50–150 room, mid-scale 150–300, luxury 100–500, mega-resort 500–2000 key; (2) Restaurant — fine dining, casual, fast-casual, café, food hall; (3) Bar / Club / Cocktail lounge; (4) Spa / Wellness; (5) Resort / Villa; (6) Hostel / Capsule hotel. Phân loại theo brand: (1) Branded chain (Marriott, Hyatt, Accor, IHG) — brand standard strict; (2) Independent boutique — creative freedom; (3) Lifestyle brand (Soho House, Standard, Ace).

Khác với Architect (building shell) — Hospitality Designer focus interior + experience. Khác với Residential Interior Designer (private home) — Hospitality scale + complexity bigger, brand + operation matter. Khác với FF&E Designer (specs furniture) — Hospitality broader. Khác với Set Designer (temporary) — Hospitality permanent. Hợp tác chặt với: Architect, Owner / Developer, Hotel Brand (nếu chain), F&B Consultant, Lighting Designer, Acoustic Designer, FF&E, Procurement.

  • Define concept + brand experience
  • Design spatial layout + flow
  • Select material, finish, color
  • Coordinate FF&E + lighting + AV

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Hospitality project workflow long — 18–60 tháng full cycle. Phase: Concept Design (4–8 tuần) → Schematic Design (8–12 tuần) → Design Development (12–20 tuần) → Construction Document (12–20 tuần) → Tender + Construction (10–24 tháng) → FF&E + Install (3–6 tháng) → Opening. Báo cáo cho Owner / Developer / Hotel Brand. Team: Lead Designer (HoD level) + Project Designer 2–5 + FF&E + drafter. Project value: $5M (small boutique) → $50M+ (mega resort).

02Công việc của một Hospitality Designer

1. Brand & concept development

Hospitality project = brand. Define brand DNA: target guest persona, positioning ("Indochinese romance", "Japanese minimalist wellness", "Tropical surf cool"), unique selling proposition. Develop narrative: each space tell story chapter. Build concept book: mood, sketch, reference, key idea. Present tới Owner — may iterate 5–15 round.

  • Brand DNA define

2. Spatial design & guest journey

Plan floor plan theo guest journey: arrival → lobby → check-in → vertical (elevator) → corridor → room. Each touchpoint design intentional. For hotel: room type (standard, deluxe, suite) plan + furniture layout. For restaurant: FOH (front of house — dining) vs BOH (back of house — kitchen, storage), table spacing (intimacy vs capacity). Comply code (fire, ADA accessibility, building local), brand standard (Marriott Brand Standards = book +1000 page).

03Hospitality Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Interior + spatial design
📋 Hospitality operation literacy
🛠️ AutoCAD, Revit, Rhino, SketchUp
✨ Brand + storytelling
💬 Client + vendor management
⚡ Detail + project management
Interior + spatial design

Foundation. Hiểu: space planning, scale, proportion, circulation, material, color theory, lighting design. Visit great hotel/restaurant — sketch, photo, analyze layout + finish choice. Read: "Hotel Design, Planning and Development" (Penner), "Restaurant Design" (Roberto Beretta).

Hospitality operation literacy

Hospitality design only success when work for operation. Hiểu: housekeeping flow (room turnover speed), F&B kitchen (line cook station), front desk (check-in efficiency), bar (bartender movement). Talk hotel manager + chef + bartender. Some design course visit running hotel for shadow program.

AutoCAD, Revit, Rhino, SketchUp

Industry standard: AutoCAD (2D drawing), Revit (BIM — large project), Rhino / SketchUp (3D modeling), V-Ray / Enscape / Lumion (rendering), Adobe InDesign + Photoshop (presentation). For luxury: hand sketch + watercolor presentation still valued.

Brand + storytelling

Hospitality = experience brand. Hiểu hospitality brand: Aman (zen Asia luxury), Six Senses (sustainable wellness), Soho House (member club), Ace (creative class), Edition (Studio 54 chic). Each have distinct identity. Study brand book — how communicate identity through space.

Client + vendor management

Stakeholder complex: Owner (rich, opinionated, time-poor), Hotel Brand exec (standard enforce), Architect (design tension), Contractor (cost concern), FF&E vendor (lead time stress). Negotiate diplomatically. Document decision rigorously.

Detail + project management

Project 18–60 tháng. Hundreds material spec, thousand FF&E item, multiple consultant. Detail obsessive. Project management tool: Asana / Monday / Procore. Document control. Construction document accurate.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Hospitality Designer?

1
Học Interior Design / Architecture 4–5 năm

Background phù hợp: Thiết kế Nội thất, Kiến trúc. VN: ĐH Kiến trúc Hà Nội/HCM, ĐH Mỹ thuật Công nghiệp, RMIT Interior Design. Quốc tế: Pratt Institute, Parsons, NYSID New York School of Interior Design, RCA London, Cornell Hotel School (rare hospitality-specific degree), Glion + Les Roches (hospitality school có design focus).

2
Junior at hospitality design firm

Apply firm chuyên hospitality. International firm in VN: HBA Hirsch Bedner Associates, Wilson Associates, Wimberly Interiors, BLINK Design, AvroKO, Yabu Pushelberg. VN firm: NU Studio, VTN Architects, HMA Architects, Mia Design Studio. Tasks: draft, material research, sample chase.

3
Mid Designer — own portion

2–4 năm — own portion of project: spa, restaurant, club lounge, room mockup. Travel showroom. Develop hospitality-specific knowledge.

4
Senior Designer / Project Designer

5–8 năm — own full project (small boutique, restaurant). Lead client meeting. Coordinate consultant team. Build portfolio 5–10 project.

5
Design Director / Studio Lead

8–12 năm — Design Director. Lead multiple project, develop design philosophy, mentor team. Recognized industry award (AHEAD Awards, Boutique Design Awards).

6
Sự nghiệp Hospitality Designer

Long-term path: (1) Partner / Principal tại firm; (2) Found own studio (VN: NU Studio, MORI Design; international: many founder route); (3) In-house Design Director tại hotel group (Marriott Design Group, IHG, Aman); (4) Independent consultant high-profile client; (5) Crossover commercial, residential, retail; (6) Educator at design school. Lương: VN junior 15–25M/tháng; mid 25–55M; senior 55M–150M; Director 150–400M+/tháng; Partner equity-based. International: $60K-$120K mid; $130K-$250K senior; Director $250K-$500K+; principal partner $500K-$2M+. Strong demand SE Asia với hospitality boom.
