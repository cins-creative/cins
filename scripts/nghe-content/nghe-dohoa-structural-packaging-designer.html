02Công việc của một Structural Packaging Designer

1. Brief + research + concept

Brief receive: product spec (dimension, weight, shape), brand vision, target market, budget, sustainability goal. Product analysis: hands-on with actual product, understand fragility + storage need. Reference + benchmark: competitor packaging, award-winning structural (Dieline, Pentawards). Material consideration: paper, plastic, metal, glass, eco-friendly alternative. Concept sketch + ideation: multiple direction.

2. 3D model + die-cut develop

03Structural Packaging Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎁 3D thinking + form
💻 ArtiosCAD + Esko
📦 Material + manufacturing
✂️ Prototype + craft
🌱 Sustainability literacy
🏭 Production process
3D thinking + form

Spatial visualization. Mental rotation. Flat to 3D unfolding. Industrial design + architecture background helpful. Read: "Packaging Design" (Klimchuk + Krasovec), "The Packaging Designer's Book of Patterns" (Roth + Wybenga).

ArtiosCAD + Esko

ArtiosCAD (Esko) industry standard structural design. SolidWorks + Rhino for complex 3D. KASEMAKE alternative. Illustrator + Photoshop for graphic. KeyShot for render.

Material + manufacturing

Paper grade (corrugated, SBS, cellulose), plastic (PET, PP, PE, PLA), metal (aluminum, tin), glass. Manufacturing — die-cut, blister, injection mold, blow mold, thermoform. Visit factory regularly.

Prototype + craft

Hands-on prototype skill — cut, fold, glue, score. Laser cutter + paper engineering tool. Visit pop-up book exhibition + Robert Sabuda paper engineer inspiration.

Sustainability literacy

Eco-friendly material (mycelium, mushroom, seaweed). Recyclable design. FSC certification. Circular economy. EU PPWR regulation. Read: "Sustainable Packaging" (Boylston).

Production process

Hiểu printing (offset, flexo, digital). Finishing (foil stamp, emboss, deboss, UV varnish, soft touch). Die-cut + creasing. Filling + sealing line at factory.
