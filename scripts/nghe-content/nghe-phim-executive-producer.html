01Executive Producer là ai?

Executive Producer (EP) là người financing/leadership cao nhất trên phim. Role này có 3 variant: (1) Studio EP — exec tại studio (Marvel's Kevin Feige, Pixar's CCO), oversee multi-project; (2) Financier EP — person/company put money in, get credit + back-end (often không creative involvement); (3) Talent EP credit — star/director given EP credit as deal sweetener (Brad Pitt EP nhiều phim mà ông không produce thực). Trong VN industry: EP thường = người raise money + executive oversight.

Khác với Producer (hands-on day-to-day production — hire crew, budget, schedule, set logistics) — EP focus tier above. Khác với Line Producer (focus budget execution) — EP focus business strategy. Khác với Showrunner (TV — combine writer/EP, very hands-on) — Film EP rộng hơn nhưng less daily. Có sub-specialty: Studio EP, Independent EP, TV Showrunner-EP, Documentary EP.

  • Raise financing — investor, studio, fund
  • Sign-off big creative + business decision
  • Manage investor/studio relationship
  • Provide strategic guidance cho Producer

Executive Producer — top financing + leadership role

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

EP ở top of production hierarchy. Báo cáo cho Studio CEO / Board / Investor. Oversee Producer (who run day-to-day), Director (creative). Phối hợp với Financier, Studio exec, Distributor, Sales agent, Talent agent. Tham gia: development (raise money — 6 tháng-2 năm trước shoot), production (oversight, big decision), post (final sign-off + marketing), release (campaign approval). Multiple parallel project — EP thường có 3–10 project active simultaneously.

02Công việc của một Executive Producer

1. Financing & deal structuring

EP raise tiền cho project. Source: studio finance (negotiate với studio greenlight committee), private investor, government fund (VN: Quỹ hỗ trợ phát triển điện ảnh; Korea: KOFIC; France: CNC), international co-production, pre-sale (sell territory right trước shoot), tax credit (UK, Canada, Georgia film tax credit). Structure complex deal — debt, equity, gap financing, completion bond.

  • Investor outreach
  • Deal structuring — multi-source financing
  • Negotiation — term, rev share

EP là bridge giữa creative team và money. Update investor / studio: weekly status, monthly financial report, milestone communication. Manage expectation: budget overrun, schedule slip, creative pivot. Defend project khi exec push back. Long-term relationship building — investor invest trong người, không project. EP với strong track record raise easier next time.

Business & deal making

EP core skill = business. Hiểu deep: deal structure (debt vs equity), tax credit, rev share, back-end, completion bond. Read "All You Need to Know About the Movie Business" (Litwak), "The Business of Film". MBA / Law background giúp ích. Learn from senior EP via mentorship / observe meeting.

Negotiation skill foundational — most EP day talking deal.

Network — financier, talent, studio

EP business is network. Build relationship qua decade: financier (private equity, family office, hedge fund), talent agent (CAA, WME, UTA), studio exec, festival programmer, distribution exec. Attend industry event: Cannes Marché du Film, AFM, Sundance, EFM Berlin. VN: Liên hoan Phim VN, HANIFF, regional event.

Strategic thinking & risk

EP make big strategic bet: which project greenlight, when release, how distribute. Long-term thinking — career build qua decade of choice. Risk tolerance high but calibrated. Study top EP track record: Kevin Feige (Marvel), Megan Ellison (Annapurna), Ted Sarandos (Netflix), Bùi Thạc Chuyên (VN).

Vision & taste — pick winner

EP pick project — picking is hardest skill. Develop taste qua: watch thousand film, study which become hit / classic / failure, identify pattern. Best EP có distinctive taste (Megan Ellison's experimental film, Plan B's mid-budget original).

Hiểu industry mechanism

EP must understand: festival circuit, distribution window, marketing economic, streaming economic shift, international market. Industry change rapidly — streaming era đã transform model. Read trade daily (Variety, Hollywood Reporter, Screen Daily, BoF for fashion crossover).

Resilience & long-game

Film industry brutal — most project don't get made, most made don't make money. EP must persist through countless "no", multiple failure, long stretches of nothing. Career take 15–25 năm to top. Develop emotional resilience.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Executive Producer?

1
Học film business / law / finance 4–5 năm

Background phù hợp: Film Business, Entertainment Law, Finance / MBA. VN: ĐH KHXH&NV (Điện ảnh), ĐH Luật (Luật Sở hữu Trí tuệ), ĐH Ngoại thương / Kinh tế. Quốc tế: USC Peter Stark Producing program, UCLA Anderson MBA-Film, NYU Stern MBA (entertainment track). Plus: watch hàng nghìn phim + read trade daily.

2
Entry: assistant tại studio / production company

Path entry: Assistant to Producer / Studio Executive. Read script, take note, manage calendar, learn deal terminology. 2–4 năm assistant trước promote. VN: assistant tại Galaxy Studio, BHD, Mega GS, Saigon Film. Hollywood: assistant tại WME, CAA, A24, Plan B, Studio.

3
Junior Producer / Coordinator

Promote thành Production Coordinator / Junior Producer. Work on actual production — learn budget, schedule, on-set issue. 3–5 năm — work on 5–10 project (short, feature, TV). Build credit.

4
Producer — own project end-to-end

Step up thành Producer. Own project: develop script, attach talent, raise budget, produce, deliver. Indie low-budget first ($500K-2M), build track record. 1–3 successful Producer credit before EP role.

5
Executive Producer role

Path tới EP: (1) Founder own production company (Plan B = Brad Pitt, A24 = Daniel Katz; VN: Đặng Tất Bình, Bùi Thạc Chuyên with own company); (2) Studio exec position (VP Development → Senior VP → President); (3) Hire/credit on specific project by financier; (4) Showrunner TV (writer/EP combo). Different path each different skill emphasis.

6
Sự nghiệp Executive Producer

Long-term path: (1) Established EP với multiple project + track record; (2) Studio chief / President of Production (Kevin Feige Marvel, Donna Langley Universal); (3) Founder mini-studio (Megan Ellison Annapurna, Brad Pitt Plan B); (4) Streaming exec (Netflix, Apple TV+ — content acquisition top role); (5) Investor/fund manager film fund. Lương: VN EP indie 300M–1 tỷ/project + back-end; studio EP 100–500M/tháng; international EP Marvel level $5M-30M/năm; top studio chief $20M+/năm + bonus. Highly powerful role nhưng take 20–30 năm to reach.
