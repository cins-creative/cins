Landscape Architect (Kiến trúc sư cảnh quan) là nghệ sĩ thiết kế không gian ngoài trời — kết hợp thực vật, đất, nước, đá, kiến trúc, lighting. 01Landscape Architect là ai?

Landscape Architect (Kiến trúc sư cảnh quan) là chuyên gia thiết kế không gian ngoài trời + cảnh quan ở mọi scale. Phân loại theo loại project: (1) Public space LA — công viên, quảng trường, đường phố, waterfront, riverwalk; (2) Residential LA — vườn nhà riêng, sân vườn villa, biệt thự; (3) Commercial / Mixed-use LA — landscape cho mall, office, mixed-use development; (4) Hospitality LA — resort, hotel garden, golf course; (5) Urban Planning LA — master plan đô thị, green network, blue infrastructure; (6) Restoration / Ecological LA — wetland, mangrove, brown field restoration. Specialization: tropical landscape đặc thù VN.

Khác với Architect (kiến trúc tòa nhà) — Landscape Architect không gian ngoài trời. Khác với Gardener / Horticulturist (trồng + chăm cây) — LA design + plan. Khác với Urban Planner (zoning + transportation) — LA detail cảnh quan. Khác với Interior Designer (indoor) — LA outdoor. Hợp tác chặt với: Architect, Urban Planner, Civil Engineer, Horticulturist, Lighting Designer, Irrigation Engineer, Contractor, Government planning agency.

  • Design cảnh quan + công viên
  • Plan plant species + soil + water
  • Integrate kiến trúc + ánh sáng
  • Consider sustainability + climate

02Công việc của một Landscape Architect

1. Site analysis + concept

Visit site multiple time. Document: topography (slope, elevation), climate (sun path, wind, rainfall), soil + drainage, existing vegetation + ecology, view + sight line, infrastructure (utility, road, building). Develop concept: design narrative, mood board, plant palette, material palette. Sketch + present to client.

  • Site analysis thorough
  • Concept + narrative
  • Present client

03Landscape Architect cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Design sense + landscape composition
🌱 Plant + horticulture knowledge
📐 Topography + site engineering
🛠️ Tool: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Rhino, Lumion, GIS
🌍 Sustainability + ecology
💬 Client + stakeholder communication
Design sense + landscape composition

Eye for outdoor design — line, form, texture, color of landscape. Study master: Roberto Burle Marx (Brazilian — tropical king), Frederick Law Olmsted (Central Park), Piet Oudolf (High Line, perennial planting), Kongjian Yu (Sponge City), Vietnam: Vo Trong Nghia (bamboo + green).

Plant + horticulture knowledge

Foundation. Hundreds of plant — name (Latin + common), growth habit, light + water requirement, mature size, seasonal interest. Tropical VN: bằng lăng, sao đen, lát hoa, ngọc lan, hoa giấy, dương xỉ, cau cảnh, bàng Đài Loan, lộc vừng. Build personal plant library qua plant visit, nursery tour, encyclopedia. Read: "Tropical Plants for Home and Garden".

Topography + site engineering

Understand grading, drainage, slope stability. Read topo map. Plan stormwater management. Calculate cut + fill earthwork. Basic structural understanding cho retaining wall, pergola.

Tool: AutoCAD, SketchUp, Rhino, Lumion, GIS

AutoCAD + Civil 3D — drafting + grading. SketchUp + Rhino — 3D modeling, fluid form. Lumion + Enscape + Twinmotion — real-time render landscape (excellent for vegetation library). Land F/X — landscape-specific CAD plugin. GIS (QGIS, ArcGIS) — large-scale + urban project. Photoshop, InDesign — presentation.

Sustainability + ecology

Modern landscape architecture = sustainability-driven. Understand: native vs invasive species, biodiversity, urban heat island, stormwater management (rain garden, bioswale), permeable paving, low-water (xeriscape). Certification: SITES, LEED, WELL. Tropical VN climate adaptation.

Client + stakeholder communication

Project public space involve government, community, developer. Communicate clearly. Run public consultation. Defend design rationale. Manage long timeline (plant take year to mature — vision long-term).

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Landscape Architect?

1
Học Kiến trúc Cảnh quan 4–5 năm

Background: Kiến trúc Cảnh quan / Landscape Architecture. VN: ĐH Kiến trúc TP.HCM/HN — Khoa Cảnh quan, ĐH Lâm nghiệp (cảnh quan), ĐH Nông Lâm. Quốc tế: Harvard GSD, UPenn, LSU, Cornell, UC Berkeley, Lincoln University (NZ), Wageningen (NL). Plus: book "Landscape Architecture" (Norman Booth), "Planting Design Illustrated".

2
Intern / Junior tại firm

VN firm có landscape division: Vo Trong Nghia Architects, SCDA, NAG Studio, JTL, Belt Collins Vietnam, Out-2 Design. International: Sasaki, OLIN, Hargreaves, SWA, James Corner Field Operations, Turenscape. Junior: drafting, plant research, project support.

3
Mid-level LA

3-6 năm — lead small-medium project. Develop concept. Run construction document. Visit site. Specialize: residential garden, resort, urban park, ecological.

4
Senior / Associate

6-12 năm — Senior LA. Lead large project. Develop own design philosophy. Mentor. Lead pitch. Manage client relationship.

5
Principal / Studio Owner

12-20 năm — Principal at large firm hoặc start own studio. VN landscape architect often partner với architect for cross-discipline studio (architecture + landscape).

6
Sự nghiệp Landscape Architect

Long-term path: (1) Studio principal — boutique firm, signature work; (2) Design director at large firm; (3) Government consultant — urban green plan; (4) Academic + researcher — ĐH Kiến trúc faculty; (5) Sustainability + ecological specialist — wetland, climate adaptation; (6) Resort + hospitality specialist — high-end resort cảnh quan (VN có market mạnh). Lương: VN LA junior 12-22M/tháng; mid 22-50M; senior 50M-120M; Principal 120M-300M+. International: $60K-$100K mid; $100K-$170K senior; Principal $170K-$350K. Demand tăng tại VN với urbanization, climate change awareness, premium real estate market. Long-term meaningful career.
