"Parasite" của Bong Joon-ho gây xúc động toàn cầu — script là foundation. "Bố Già" Trấn Thành chạm trái tim 400 tỷ box office — vì story tốt. "The Godfather" became iconic — vì Mario Puzo + Francis Ford Coppola wrote masterful screenplay.

Screenwriter là chuyên gia viết kịch bản phim (screenplay) — foundation của mọi phim. Là chuyên gia kể chuyện qua dialogue + action + structure. Oscar Best Screenplay (Original + Adapted) tôn vinh role này. Without good script, no good movie — regardless director + actor + production talent.

01Screenwriter phim là ai?

Screenwriter là chuyên gia tạo screenplay. Phân loại theo project: (1) Feature screenwriter — film 90-180 min; (2) TV writer — episode 22-60 min, often in writers' room; (3) Short film writer — under 40 min; (4) Streaming series — Netflix, Apple, Amazon original; (5) Documentary writer — narration, structure. Phân loại theo origin: (1) Original screenplay — writer's own idea; (2) Adaptation — from book, true story, comic, video game; (3) Rewrite / polish — script doctor work. Phân loại theo role: (1) Sole writer; (2) Writing partner (Coen brother, Russo brother); (3) Writers' room (TV — Showrunner + Staff Writer + Story Editor + Producer); (4) Script doctor — rewrite specialist.

Khác với Director (visual + execution) — Screenwriter foundation. Khác với Producer (business + finance) — Screenwriter creative origin. Khác với Novelist (book form) — Screenwriter cinematic-specific. Khác với Playwright (theater) — Screenwriter film/TV. Khác với Story Editor (TV editing/development) — sometime same person. Hợp tác chặt với: Producer, Director, Studio executive / development exec, Agent + manager, Showrunner (TV), Writers' room peers (TV).

1. Concept + outline + treatment

Concept develop: logline (1-2 sentence), premise, theme. Outline: story structure — 3-act, 5-act, save the cat beat sheet, sequence approach. Treatment: 5-30 page prose summary — used để pitch. Character development: backstory, want, need, flaw, arc. Research: subject matter expertise. Beat sheet: scene-by-scene outline.

03Screenwriter phim cần giỏi gì?

📖 Storytelling mastery
🎭 Character + dialogue
🎬 Cinematic + visual sense
🛠️ Final Draft + format
💼 Business + relationship
💪 Resilience + rejection
Storytelling mastery

Structure: 3-act, 5-act, hero's journey. Beat. Subplot. Theme. Read foundational: "Story" (McKee), "Save the Cat!" (Snyder), "The Anatomy of Story" (Truby), "Writing Down the Bones" (Goldberg).

Character + dialogue

Three-dimensional character. Distinct voice. Subtext. Active vs passive. Read: "Creating Characters" (William Bernhardt), Aaron Sorkin screenplay, "On Writing" (Stephen King).

Cinematic + visual sense

Watch movie obsessively. Read screenplay. Hiểu film grammar — cut, montage, sequence. Write visual not literary. Read classic screenplay (PDF available — go to scriptsource).

Final Draft + format

Industry tool: Final Draft (industry standard), WriterDuet, Celtx, Highland, Fade In. Format strict — wrong format = unread.

Business + relationship

Industry navigate. Agent + manager relationship. Producer contact. Network. Pitch craft. Read: "Adventures in the Screen Trade" (Goldman), "The Hollywood Writers' Wars" (Schwartz).

Resilience + rejection

Rejection 99%+ pitch. Years between sale. Stay positive + keep writing. Spec script after script. Long-term game. Read: "Big Magic" (Gilbert), "The War of Art" (Pressfield).

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Screenwriter phim?

1
Học Screenwriting / Film / Literature / any 3–4 năm

Background: Screenwriting, Film Studies, Creative Writing, Literature, Theater. VN: ĐH SKĐA TP.HCM / Hà Nội, RMIT Creative Writing, ĐH KHXH&NV Văn học. Quốc tế: USC School of Cinematic Arts Screenwriting, NYU Tisch Dramatic Writing, UCLA Screenwriting, AFI Screenwriting, Columbia MFA. Plus self-study + write constantly.

2
Write spec script 1–5 năm

Write 5-10 spec script. Develop voice + range. Submit to competition: Nicholl Fellowship, Austin Film Festival, BlueCat Screenplay, PAGE International, Sundance Writers Lab. Get into Black List ranking. VN: HFF (Hanoi Int'l Film Festival), DANAFF Da Nang Asian Film Festival emerging writer track.

3
Get representation (Agent + Manager) 2–5 năm

Manager first typically (more accessible than agent). Agent: WME, CAA, UTA, ICM. Manager: many shop. Relationship critical for sale.

4
First sale / TV staff 3–8 năm

First spec sale (huge milestone). Option deal common. TV staff writer entry — Showrunner-Mentee.

5
Working writer / Showrunner 8–20 năm

Multiple sale. TV Showrunner. Feature directing crossover. Build franchise.

6
Sự nghiệp Screenwriter phim

Long-term path: (1) A-list screenwriter — Aaron Sorkin, Charlie Kaufman, Greta Gerwig, Bong Joon-ho; (2) Writer-Director — most influential path; (3) Showrunner — TV creator + executive producer; (4) Script doctor specialist — high-pay rewrite work; (5) Novelist / playwright crossover; (6) Producer + Director ownership; (7) Educator + book author. Lương: VN screenwriter early 30-100M/script; mid 100-300M/script; established 300M-1B+/script; TV writer 15-50M/episode. International: $50K-$150K per script entry; WGA scale $80K+ feature minimum; A-list $1M-$5M per script; TV writers' room $5K-$30K/week. Highly variable income — feast or famine. Successful career means 1-2 sale per năm. Lifetime body of work matter — career compound through reputation.
