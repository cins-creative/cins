Khi VFX team Marvel có 3,000 VFX shot across 8 vendor studios + 2 năm production, khi Avatar 2 với 3,500+ shot trên multiple studio across continent, khi Vietnamese VFX project tại Saigon Animation + Bad Clay đồng thời với US client — đó là tác phẩm của VFX Coordinator.

VFX Coordinator là chuyên gia quản lý daily logistic của VFX team — shot list, asset tracking, vendor coordinate, schedule, budget tracking. Báo cáo VFX Producer. Entry-to-mid production management role trong VFX pipeline. Bridge giữa creative team + production management. Without Coordinator, large VFX project chaotic + miss deadline.

01VFX Coordinator là ai?

VFX Coordinator là production management role tại VFX studio / production company. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Studio-side VFX Coordinator — work tại VFX vendor (ILM, Weta, DNEG); (2) Production-side VFX Coordinator — work tại film production company, coordinate với vendor; (3) VFX Production Assistant — entry-level; (4) Show / Project Coordinator — per project; (5) Department Coordinator — per VFX department (rotoscoping, CG, comp). Phân loại theo career: (1) VFX Production Assistant 0-2 năm; (2) VFX Coordinator 2-5 năm; (3) Senior VFX Coordinator 5-8 năm; (4) VFX Producer; (5) Executive VFX Producer. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Major VFX studio — ILM, Weta, DNEG, Framestore, MPC, Method, Rodeo FX; (2) Boutique VFX studio — Bad Clay, Vfxhive; (3) Production company; (4) Animation studio (VFX-adjacent).

Khác với VFX Producer — Coordinator support Producer, Producer overall responsibility. Coordinator entry-mid, Producer senior. Khác với VFX Supervisor — Supervisor creative + technical, Coordinator logistical + management. Khác với VFX Artist — Artist make VFX, Coordinator manage process. Khác với Production Coordinator (film overall) — Film Coordinator broader, VFX Coordinator VFX-specific. Hợp tác chặt với: VFX Producer (direct manager), VFX Supervisor, VFX Artist team, Editor, Director + Producer (production side), Vendor + outsource studio (when applicable), Finance + Legal.

02Công việc của một VFX Coordinator

1. Shot list + asset tracking

Foundation. Shot list build: ShotGrid (Autodesk Flow) / ftrack / Aspera tracking. Every VFX shot logged với detail (length, complexity, status). Asset library track: model, texture, rig, animation cache — version history. Status update: artist daily update, Coordinator compile. Bid pack manage: VFX bid for production, breakdown per shot.

03VFX Coordinator cần giỏi gì?

📊 Organization + detail
💻 ShotGrid + ftrack + tool
🎬 VFX pipeline literacy
💬 Communication multi-stakeholder
🌐 Multi-language + cross-cultural
⚡ Calm under pressure
Organization + detail

Spreadsheet master. Database thinking. Detail obsession. Track 100-1,000+ shot status simultaneously. Read: "Getting Things Done" (Allen), "The Checklist Manifesto" (Gawande).

ShotGrid + ftrack + tool

ShotGrid (Autodesk Flow) industry standard. ftrack alternative. Aspera file transfer large. Frame.io review. Slack communication. Excel + Google Sheets advanced.

VFX pipeline literacy

Hiểu VFX department (matchmove, roto, paint, CG, FX, comp). Hiểu artist workflow. Speak VFX language. Read: "The VES Handbook of Visual Effects".

Communication multi-stakeholder

Artist (tech), Supervisor (creative), Producer (business), Client (formal), Vendor (cross-culture). Adapt tone per audience.

Multi-language + cross-cultural

English mandatory. Local language helpful (Vietnamese cho VN studio). Cultural sensitivity với international vendor. Timezone juggling skill.

Calm under pressure

Crunch time normal. Last-minute change. Crisis daily. Composure essential. Conflict mediate. Long hour ability.
