03Storyboard Artist illustration cần giỏi gì?

✏️ Drawing + style range
🎬 Cinematography literacy
📱 iPad + Procreate
📖 Storytelling concise
⚡ Speed + volume
💼 Client + agency literacy
Drawing + style range

Adapt style — realistic to cartoon to graphic. Figure drawing strong. Perspective master. Read: "Drawing on the Right Side of the Brain" (Edwards), "Force: Dynamic Life Drawing" (Mattesi), "Storyboards: Motion in Art" (Simon).

Cinematography literacy

Shot composition + camera language. Watch commercial reel + music video reference. Read: "The Filmmaker's Eye" (Mercado), "The Five C's of Cinematography" (Mascelli).

iPad + Procreate

iPad + Procreate primary tool. Photoshop + Sketchbook. Storyboarder (free) for layout. Toon Boom Storyboard Pro for film. Keynote / PowerPoint for deck.

Storytelling concise

15-30 second commercial — every panel count. Beat + story compress. Visual hook. Read: "Hey Whipple, Squeeze This" (Sullivan, advertising).

Speed + volume

Commercial deadline tight. 15-50 panel/dự án. Multiple project parallel. Comfortable với rapid iteration + revision.

Client + agency literacy

Agency workflow. Creative brief reading. Brand guideline respect. Client diplomacy. Pitching process. Read: "The Idea Writers" (Iezzi).
