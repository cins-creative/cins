Khi Netflix decide greenlight script Squid Game thay vì 1000 script khác, khi Marvel Studios shape Avengers Endgame script qua 100+ draft, khi A24 develop Everything Everywhere All At Once từ rough pitch thành Oscar Best Picture — đó là tác phẩm của Story Developer (Development Executive).

Story Developer là chuyên gia đánh giá + develop script + concept cho studio + production company. Là gatekeeper + creative shepherd của development phase trước greenlight. Read 100s of script per năm, identify gem, work với writer để improve. Critical role mà audience không thấy, nhưng define which film get made.

01Story Developer phim là ai?

Story Developer (Development Executive / Story Editor / Development Producer) là chuyên gia development phase. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Studio — Universal, Disney, Warner Bros Story Development team; (2) Streamer — Netflix, Amazon, Apple Story team; (3) Production company — Plan B, Bad Robot, Annapurna; (4) Producer's company; (5) Talent agency — UTA, CAA — agent có story executive. Phân loại theo career: (1) Story Assistant / Reader — entry, read script; (2) Story Editor; (3) Director of Development; (4) VP / SVP Production / Development; (5) President / Head of Production. Phân loại theo content type: (1) Film; (2) TV scripted; (3) Unscripted; (4) Animation.

Khác với Screenwriter (write script) — Story Developer evaluate + guide writer. Khác với Producer (greenlight + execute) — Story Developer focus on script + development, may transition to Producer. Khác với Story Editor trên show — staff TV writer's room role, different. Khác với Reader / Coverage Writer — Reader = entry-level Story Developer track. Hợp tác chặt với: Screenwriter, Director (attached), Producer, Studio executive, Agent + Manager (writer rep), Acquisitions team (rights + IP).

  • Script + concept evaluate
  • Writer + project develop
  • Coverage + memo write
  • Greenlight prep + recommend

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Story Developer hoạt động development phase (1-5 năm before greenlight). Báo cáo Director of Development / VP Production / Head of Studio. Office work — reading + writing + meeting. Per script: 1-3 ngày read + cover. 100+ script/năm. Long-term project tracking (single project có thể 3-5 năm develop trước produce).

Daily activity. Source script: từ agent (UTA, CAA, WME), manager, writer query, contest (Nicholl, Black List, Sundance Lab), book + article (IP). Read fast + deep: 100-200 page script in 3-5 hour. Coverage write: 3-5 page report — logline, synopsis, characters, comments, recommend (PASS / CONSIDER / RECOMMEND). Categorize: priority track. Tracking board update: trong company database.

Story literacy + taste

Story structure expert (3-act, Save the Cat, Hero's Journey). Genre convention. Taste — distinguish great from good from bad. Read: "Story" (McKee), "Save the Cat" (Snyder), "The Anatomy of Story" (Truby), Black List script (year-end best unproduced).

Coverage + memo write

Concise + clear coverage. Identify essence. Recommend with conviction. Notes that help writer (not just critique). Read: "The Coverage Ink Guide", "Crafting Short Screenplays" (Cowgill).

Film + TV industry deep

Studio landscape. Box office + streamer metric. Agent + manager + producer ecosystem. Awards season. Festival. Read: Variety, Hollywood Reporter, Deadline daily. Industry book: "The Big Picture" (Belton).

Creative feedback diplomacy

Honest feedback delivered with care. Respect writer ego. Specific + actionable note. Avoid generic "I don't like it". Read: "How to Be a Producer in the New Film and TV Industry" (Manderley).

Network + relationship

Agent + manager + writer + director + producer network. Long-term relationship build. Generous (help when no immediate return). LA + NYC industry culture.

Business + budget literacy

Estimate budget. P&L (Profit & Loss) understanding. Audience + market segmentation. Streamer vs theatrical economic. Marketing perspective.
