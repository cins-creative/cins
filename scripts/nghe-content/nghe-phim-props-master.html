Indiana Jones whip, Harry Potter wand, Lord of the Rings One Ring, Pulp Fiction briefcase glowing, Wolverine claws, Batman Batarang — đó là những prop iconic của lịch sử film. Người phụ trách tạo + maintain + deploy chúng = Props Master.

Props Master phim là chuyên gia quản lý toàn bộ prop (đạo cụ) trong phim — tìm + tạo + maintain + on-set deploy. Là creative + logistics + craft role hybrid. Critical role — prop misplace = entire scene re-shoot. Iconic prop = lifetime cultural impact + franchise revenue.

01Props Master phim là ai?

Props Master là chuyên gia department head cho Props department trong film production. Phân loại theo project type: (1) Feature film Props Master; (2) TV series Props Master; (3) Commercial Props Master; (4) Music video / theater Props Master. Phân loại theo specialty: (1) Period / historical — accuracy critical (15th century to 1980s); (2) Contemporary; (3) Fantasy / sci-fi — invent new world; (4) Documentary; (5) Action / weapon-heavy — gun, sword, fight prop. Phân loại theo team size: (1) Solo (indie) — Props Master alone; (2) Department — multiple Prop Assistant + buyer + maker.

Khác với Production Designer (overall visual world) — Props Master physical prop specialty. Khác với Art Director (set + Production Designer's deputy) — Props Master prop specialty. Khác với Set Decorator (set dressing — non-touching object placement) — Props Master hero prop (actor handle). Khác với Costume Designer (wardrobe). Khác với Weapons Master (firearm sub-specialty). Hợp tác chặt với: Production Designer (boss), Director + DP, Set Decorator, Actor (prop ergonomics), Costume + MU, 1st AD + script supervisor.

  • All prop find + create
  • On-set deploy + maintain
  • Continuity + safety oversight
  • Department team lead

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Props Master work tại film + TV production. Báo cáo Production Designer / Art Director. Manage team: 2-15 Prop Assistant + buyer + maker + on-set Prop. Work entire production cycle: prep (4-12 tuần), shoot, wrap. Field-heavy + workshop-heavy job. On-set during shoot — long hour, physical, focus.

02Công việc của một Props Master phim

1. Script breakdown + prop list

Pre-production. Script breakdown: identify every prop mentioned + implied. Prop list: per scene, per character, per type. Reference research: period accuracy (1920s phone differs 1940s), brand recognition (Coca-Cola period bottle). Source plan: buy off-shelf, rent (prop house), custom-make, repurpose, hero (special). Budget allocate per prop type.

  • Script breakdown
  • Prop list + reference
  • Source plan

3. On-set deploy + actor handoff

Shoot day. On-set Prop: deploy prop at each location, scene. Actor handoff: brief actor on prop use, weight, handling, safety. Continuity: log prop state per take (used, position, condition) — script supervisor coordinate. Reset between take: position correct. Emergency repair: prop break, fix immediately to keep shoot moving. Action prop safety: weapon, sharp, breakable — coordinate với Stunt + Safety.

03Props Master phim cần giỏi gì?

🔍 Research + period accuracy
🔨 Craft + fabrication
📋 Project management
🛡️ Safety + handling
🎬 Film production know-how
👥 Team management
Research + period accuracy

Deep historical + cultural research. Period-correct prop (1920s ≠ 1940s). Brand authentic. Reference book + museum + archive access. Specialty knowledge for period drama, sci-fi, fantasy.

Craft + fabrication

Sculpting, fabrication, machining, sewing, mold-making. Material knowledge: foam, resin, metal, wood, fabric. Tools: CNC, 3D printing, laser cutter, traditional craft. Increasingly tech-savvy với 3D printing standard.

Project management

Multiple prop parallel. Tool: Excel, Smartsheet, prop-specific software. Budget tracking. Vendor management. Inventory.

Safety + handling

Weapon, sharp, breakable, fire, chemical. OSHA compliance. Coordinate with Stunt + Safety. Insurance know. Liability awareness.

Film production know-how

Set protocol. Continuity. Camera-ready prop (one side might face camera). Light + color awareness (prop look different on camera).

Team management

Lead department. Hire + manage Prop Assistant + buyer + maker. Long hour together — team chemistry. Mentor career.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Props Master phim?

1
Học Theater / Film Production / Art / Fabrication 3–4 năm

Background: Theater Tech, Film Production, Industrial Art, Sculpture, Fabrication. VN: ĐH SKĐA TP.HCM / Hà Nội, ĐH Mỹ thuật Công nghiệp. Quốc tế: USC Cinematic Arts, NYU Tisch, AFI, RIT Industrial Art, NYU MFA Stage Properties. Theater background classic path — many cross over from theater.

2
Prop Assistant 2–4 năm

Entry as Prop Assistant on film. Learn department. VN production: Galaxy, BHD, CJ HK, Mar6 Pictures; international shoot in VN (Kong: Skull Island, A Tourist's Guide to Love). Network với existing Props Master.

3
Prop Maker / On-set Prop 4–7 năm

Specialty (fabrication or on-set). Build portfolio.

4
Props Master 7–12 năm

Department head. Indie film first → bigger budget project. Build reputation cho specialty.

5
Senior Props Master / specialty studio 12+ năm

Senior level. Award-quality work. Specialty studio own (e.g., movie prop fabrication business).

6
Sự nghiệp Props Master phim

Long-term path: (1) A-list Props Master — Marvel, Star Wars, big franchise; (2) Production Designer crossover; (3) Own prop fabrication studio — service multiple production; (4) Theme park / themed entertainment — Disney, Universal Studios; (5) Educator + workshop founder; (6) Movie memorabilia / auction — iconic prop sell for $100K+ at auction. Lương: VN Props Master 25-60M/film (small/mid project); 60-150M (major). International: $3,000-$6,000/week mid; $6,000-$12,000/week senior; major franchise $500K+ year. Some legendary Props Master become household name (e.g., John Mollo for Star Wars).
