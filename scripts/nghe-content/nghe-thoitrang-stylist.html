03Stylist thời trang cần giỏi gì?

👗 Fashion knowledge deep
🎨 Taste + visual eye
🤝 Network + relationship
💼 Negotiation + diplomacy
📸 Photo + media literacy
⚡ Crisis composure
Fashion knowledge deep

Designer + brand + collection encyclopedic. Fashion history (Coco Chanel, Yves Saint Laurent, Galliano era Dior). Vintage knowledge. Read: "The Beautiful Fall" (Drake), "Dior by Dior" (Christian Dior), follow Business of Fashion + Vogue Runway.

Taste + visual eye

Cultivated taste — what work, what tired, what avant-garde. Risk balance (safe vs bold). Watch best-dressed list, Met Gala, Cannes red carpet. Study Vogue cover history.

Network + relationship

Designer + PR + showroom long-term relationship. Reciprocity (loan today, place piece in future shoot). Fashion industry small + relational. Discretion (handle client privacy).

Negotiation + diplomacy

Negotiate loan terms, custom commission, deadline. Diplomatic với conflicting opinion (client + manager + publicist + designer). Tactful no.

Photo + media literacy

Hiểu camera lens (how outfit photograph), lighting (color shift), red carpet position. Press coverage knowledge (best-dressed, worst-dressed list). Social media impact awareness.

Crisis composure

Outfit fail last minute, missing piece, wardrobe malfunction — solve fast. Backup ready. Calm under pressure (camera + flash + 1 hour to red carpet pressure).
