01Writer / Screenwriter hoạt hình là ai?

Writer / Screenwriter hoạt hình là chuyên gia script cho animation. Phân loại theo format: (1) Feature film Screenwriter — Pixar, Disney 90-min feature; (2) TV series Writer — episodic animation; (3) Short film Writer; (4) Game cinematic Writer; (5) Streaming animation Writer — Netflix, Disney+. Phân loại theo career: (1) Junior Writer / Staff Writer; (2) Writer; (3) Senior Writer / Story Editor; (4) Showrunner (TV) / Director-Writer (feature); (5) Showrunner + Producer. Phân loại theo writing room: (1) Solo writer — feature often solo or pair; (2) Writing room (TV) — collaborative 5-15 writer; (3) Story Department (Pixar model) — writer + storyboard artist work together. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Studio in-house — Pixar Story Department, Disney Animation Story; (2) Freelance + per project; (3) Streaming platform writing room; (4) Independent — author-driven (Hayao Miyazaki).

Khác với Live-action Screenwriter — Live-action script written first then production. Animation script + storyboard parallel iterate. Khác với Storyboard Artist — Storyboard visual, Writer text. Closely work tại Pixar model. Khác với Comic Writer — Comic for static panel, Animation for moving. Khác với Game Writer — Game branching, Animation linear. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, Storyboard Artist (Pixar model), Story Editor + Showrunner, Animation Director (creative continuity), Voice Director (dialogue), Editor, Producer (notes).

03Writer / Screenwriter hoạt hình cần giỏi gì?

📖 Story structure + craft
👥 Character + dialogue
🎬 Visual storytelling
🎨 Animation literacy
💬 Collaboration + notes
⏰ Patience + iteration
Story structure + craft

Three-act structure. Hero's Journey. Save the Cat beat. Read: "Story" (McKee), "Save the Cat!" (Snyder), "The Anatomy of Story" (Truby), "Story Sense" (Lucey).

Character + dialogue

Character voice distinct. Backstory + motivation. Subtext. Read: "Creating Characters with Charles Schulz" + "The Art of Dramatic Writing" (Egri), watch + analyze Pixar Inside Out + Up.

Visual storytelling

Show don't tell. Action over exposition. Animation strength leverage (impossible visual). Read: "The Anatomy of Story" (Truby), study Pixar shorts.

Animation literacy

Hiểu animation principle + pipeline. What animation can / can't. Genre history (Disney golden, Pixar 1990s+, anime). Read: "The Animator's Survival Kit" (Williams), "Creativity, Inc." (Catmull).

Collaboration + notes

Story Trust (Pixar internal critique culture). Take note gracefully. Kill darling. Read: "Creativity, Inc." (Catmull), "The Pixar Way" (Catmull).

Patience + iteration

Pixar feature 5-7 năm. 5-20 draft. Major story restart common. Ego control. Long-haul mindset.
