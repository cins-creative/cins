Architectural Lighting Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế chiếu sáng cho công trình + không gian. Khác với Electrical Engineer (chỉ wiring), Lighting Designer là nghệ sĩ kết hợp: kỹ thuật chiếu sáng + thẩm mỹ + hành vi người dùng. Là discipline chuyên môn cao, separate from architect + interior designer.

01Lighting Designer là ai?

Architectural Lighting Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế hệ thống chiếu sáng cho không gian + công trình. Phân loại theo project: (1) Hospitality / Retail Lighting — khách sạn, resort, restaurant, cửa hàng (hero space, atmospheric); (2) Cultural / Museum Lighting — bảo tàng, gallery, nhà thờ (color rendering accurate, exhibit-grade); (3) Commercial / Office Lighting — văn phòng (productivity + wellness focused, code compliant); (4) Residential / Luxury Lighting — villa, penthouse cao cấp; (5) Public + Urban Lighting — quảng trường, công viên, facade building, monument; (6) Theatrical Lighting — sân khấu, concert (separate but related).

Khác với Electrical Engineer (wiring, code, safety) — Lighting Designer aesthetic + experience. Khác với Interior Designer (space full) — Lighting specifically light. Khác với Architect (building shell) — Lighting layer above. Hợp tác chặt với: Architect, Interior Designer, Electrical Engineer, Project Manager, Contractor, Manufacturer / Supplier.

  • Design lighting plan + fixture selection

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Lighting Designer thường specialist consultant — không in-house ở architect firm (except large studio như Foster, Gensler). Hire bởi architect / interior designer / client. Workflow: concept phase (mood + reference 2-4 tuần) → schematic (plan + fixture selection 4-8 tuần) → design development (detail spec 4-8 tuần) → construction documents → site supervise install → focus + commissioning. Project: residential (3-6 tháng), boutique hotel (6-12 tháng), large hotel / commercial (12-24 tháng). Report Principal / Studio Lead. Phối hợp với Architect, Interior, Electrical Engineer.

02Công việc của một Lighting Designer

1. Brief + concept + mood

Meet client + architect. Understand project intent — function (productivity vs ambiance), brand identity (luxury / casual / corporate), target user. Visit site / review architectural drawing. Develop lighting concept: design narrative, mood reference, color temperature direction (warm 2700K / neutral 3500K / cool 4000K+). Present.

  • Brief + understand intent
  • Concept + mood
  • Color temperature direction

2. Layer design — ambient, task, accent

Plan 3 layer of light: (1) Ambient — general fill, ceiling lighting; (2) Task — focused work (kitchen counter, desk, reading); (3) Accent — highlight feature (art, plant, architecture element). Layer interactive — change scene from "morning bright" to "evening intimate" qua control. Calculate lux level (workplace 300-500 lux, hospitality 50-200 lux, retail 750+ at product display).

  • 3 layer design
  • Lux level per zone
  • Scene control

3. Specify luminaire + control system

Select specific luminaire (fixture) — manufacturer + model + lumen + CCT + CRI + beam angle. Spec from major brand: iGuzzini, ERCO, Flos, Bega, Artemide, Lutron. Specify control: dimming (0-10V, DALI, Casambi), scene control (Lutron, Crestron), tunable white (CCT shift). Provide fixture schedule (BOQ — bill of quantity) for contractor.

03Lighting Designer cần giỏi gì?

💡 Light theory + photometry
🎨 Aesthetic + color science
🛠️ Tool: DIALux, Relux, AGi32, AutoCAD, Revit, 3ds Max
📋 Knowledge of fixture + control
📐 Code + standard + sustainability
💬 Client + architect collaboration
Light theory + photometry

Foundation. Lumen, lux, candela, color temperature (Kelvin), CRI (color rendering index), beam angle, IES file (photometric file). Hiểu quantitative + qualitative light. Read: "Designing with Light" (Karlen), "Architectural Lighting" (Mark Karlen, James Benya).

Aesthetic + color science

Eye for atmosphere. Study how light shape mood — restaurant warm intimate, museum cool neutral, hospital functional cool. Study color science (Kruithof curve — warm light low level / cool light high level). Follow magazine: Mondo, L+A (Lighting + Architecture), Architectural Record.

Tool: DIALux, Relux, AGi32, AutoCAD, Revit, 3ds Max

DIALux evo — primary lighting calculation tool (free, industry standard EU). Relux — alternative. AGi32 — US standard. AutoCAD + Revit — coordination drawing. 3ds Max + V-Ray — high-end visualization render. Photoshop — present.

Knowledge of fixture + control

Master fixture catalog. Major brand: iGuzzini, ERCO, Flos, Bega, Artemide, Zumtobel, Vibia, Louis Poulsen, Lutron. VN local: Rạng Đông, Điện Quang, Duhal. Visit showroom. Attend exhibition Light + Building Frankfurt, EuroLuce Milan, LED+Light Asia.

Code + standard + sustainability

Code: VN QCVN, US IES, EU EN. Workplace, emergency, exit. Sustainability: LEED v4 Light Pollution + Daylight, WELL v2 Light, circadian lighting (human-centric). Energy code (W/m²).

Client + architect collaboration

Consultant role — work for + with multiple stakeholder. Listen. Explain technical clearly. Manage budget + expectation. Negotiate fee. Build long-term relationship.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Lighting Designer?

1
Học Kiến trúc / Thiết kế / Electrical Engineering 4–5 năm

Common path: Architecture, Interior Design, Electrical Engineering, Theatrical Lighting Design. VN: ĐH Kiến trúc, ĐH Bách Khoa Điện, ĐH Sân khấu Điện ảnh. Quốc tế: Parsons MFA Lighting Design, Pratt, Rensselaer Lighting Research Center, KTH (Stockholm), Hochschule Wismar (Germany). Plus: IALD professional development, certificate (LEED, WELL).

2
Junior at Lighting consultant 2–4 năm

VN lighting design firm: Lighting Design Studio, Light & Design Vietnam, NAG Studio (lighting branch), iGuzzini Vietnam, ERCO Vietnam. International: Lighting Design International, Speirs + Major, Tillotson Design, Lighting Workshop. Junior task: drafting, calculation, fixture research.

3
Mid-level Lighting Designer 4–7 năm

Lead small project. Develop concept independently. Manage client. Specialize: hospitality, retail, residential, public.

4
Senior / Associate 7–12 năm

Senior. Lead large project (hotel, public space). Develop signature style. Mentor.

5
Principal / Studio Owner 12–20 năm

Principal at firm hoặc start own consultancy. Established client base. International project.

6
Sự nghiệp Lighting Designer

Long-term path: (1) Studio principal — own consultancy, signature project; (2) In-house at architecture mega-firm — Gensler, Foster, AECOM; (3) Sales / technical support at lighting manufacturer — ERCO, iGuzzini, Lutron — high-paying; (4) Smart building / human-centric lighting specialist — emerging hot field (circadian, wellness); (5) Educator; (6) Lighting designer for film / TV / theater — adjacent industry. Lương: VN Lighting Designer junior 12-22M/tháng; mid 22-50M; senior 50M-120M; Principal 120M-300M+. International: $60K-$100K mid; $100K-$170K senior; Principal $170K-$350K. VN demand cao với hospitality + luxury real estate boom. Niche specialty + recognized profession.
