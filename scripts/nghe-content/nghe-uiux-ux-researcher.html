01UX Researcher là ai?

UX Researcher là senior research function trong design org. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Generative researcher — discover problem trước design (foundational, strategic); (2) Evaluative researcher — test design + validate (tactical); (3) Strategic researcher — market + persona + long-term direction; (4) ResearchOps specialist — tool, panel, process; (5) Mixed-method generalist. Phân loại theo career: (1) UX Researcher; (2) Senior UX Researcher; (3) Staff / Principal UX Researcher; (4) Lead / Manager; (5) Director of Research / Head of UXR. Phân loại theo focus area: (1) Consumer product UXR; (2) Enterprise / SaaS UXR; (3) Fintech / banking UXR; (4) Hardware UXR — car, smart home, VR; (5) Emerging market UXR — global consumer research. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Tech in-house — Google, Meta, Apple; (2) Research consultancy; (3) Design agency; (4) Freelance + boutique.

Khác với User Researcher — overlap large, UX Researcher tại large company senior + scope strategic, User Researcher entry-mid + tactical. Tại small company same role. Khác với Data Analyst — Data quant only + product metric, UXR qual + quant + behavior context. Khác với UX Designer — Designer design, UXR research + inform. Khác với Service Designer — Service broader (service touchpoint), UX specifically product. Hợp tác chặt với: UX + UI Designer + Product Designer, Product Manager, Data Analyst + Data Scientist, Engineer, Marketing + Customer Insights, Customer Support, Other UXR (research community).

03UX Researcher cần giỏi gì?

🔬 Research method depth
📊 Statistics + analytics
🧠 Systems thinking + strategic
📝 Writing + storytelling
💬 Stakeholder + influence
🌐 Cross-cultural + emerging market
Research method depth

Full method toolkit — interview, ethnographic, diary, survey, A/B test, card sort, tree test, eye-tracking. Bias awareness deep. Read: "Research Methods" (Babbie), "Just Enough Research" (Hall), "The User Experience Team of One" (Buley).

Statistics + analytics

Inferential statistics, regression, ANOVA. Survey analysis. A/B test analysis. R, Python, SPSS, JMP. Read: "Quantifying the User Experience" (Sauro + Lewis), "Naked Statistics" (Wheelan).

Systems thinking + strategic

Big picture. Connect research to business. Long-term roadmap. Multi-quarter perspective. Read: "Thinking in Systems" (Meadows), "Continuous Discovery Habits" (Torres).

Writing + storytelling

Comprehensive report. Executive summary punchy. Visual + data + quote combo. Narrative arc. Read: "Made to Stick" (Heath), "Storytelling with Data" (Knaflic).

Stakeholder + influence

VP-level present + push back. Build research culture. Convince busy PM. Long-term relationship. Read: "Articulating Design Decisions" (Greever), "Influence" (Cialdini).

Cross-cultural + emerging market

Multi-country research. Cultural sensitivity. Translation + local research partner. Vietnam-specific insight valuable globally (mobile-first market). Read: "The Culture Map" (Meyer).
