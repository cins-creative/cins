Re-recording Mixer là final stage của sound post-production — kết hợp dialogue, music, sound effect thành final mix theatrical / streaming. Là người shape final audio experience cho phim. Oscar Best Sound recognize role này — Re-recording Mixer là một trong sound team key role.

01Re-recording Mixer là ai?

Re-recording Mixer (còn gọi Final Mixer hoặc Dub Mixer) là chuyên gia mix final cho phim sound. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Dialogue Mixer — clean + balance dialogue; (2) Music Mixer — balance score + song; (3) Effects Mixer — balance SFX. Trong large team có separate mixer; smaller team single Mixer handle all. Phân loại theo deliverable: (1) Theatrical mix — for cinema (5.1, 7.1, Dolby Atmos); (2) Near-field mix — TV broadcast (stereo, 5.1); (3) Streaming mix — Netflix, Disney+, Amazon (specific spec each platform); (4) International mix — for dub language (M&E — Music + Effects stem).

Khác với Production Sound Mixer (on-set record) — Re-recording post-production. Khác với Sound Designer (create SFX) — Re-recording mix existing. Khác với Sound Editor (edit + cleanup dialogue + sync) — Re-recording downstream of editor. Khác với Music Composer (write music) — Re-recording mix music in. Hợp tác chặt với: Sound Designer, Sound Editor (Dialogue, ADR, Foley, FX), Music Editor, Director, Picture Editor, Composer.

  • Dialogue + music + SFX balance
  • Surround sound place

02Công việc của một Re-recording Mixer

1. Pre-mix + prep

Receive sound deliverable: dialogue stem, ADR, Foley, SFX, music — all from respective Editor. Session setup: Pro Tools session với hundreds of track organized. Pre-mix: each stem balance individually first (dialogue pre-mix, music pre-mix, FX pre-mix). Reference review: Director temp mix + intent + reference film.

2. Final mix + balance

Core craft. Balance dialogue + music + SFX: dialogue clear at all time. Music swell at emotional. SFX punctuate action. Dynamic shape: quiet + loud contrast — explosion impact require quiet before. Frequency carve: EQ each element so don't fight — music low when dialogue, SFX high freq don't mask dialogue. Reverb + space: place sound in 3D acoustic space matching scene.

  • 3-element balance
  • Dynamic shape
  • Frequency carve

3. Surround + immersive + Atmos

Surround sound placement: 5.1 (front L, C, R, sub, surround L, R), 7.1, Dolby Atmos (object-based, height channel). Spatial design: ambient surround place audience inside scene. Pan + movement: car drive across screen → sound pan accordingly. LFE (low frequency effect): explosion + bass tasteful. Height channel Atmos: rain, helicopter overhead.

4. Director collaborate + deliverable

Director review: Director sit in mix stage, give feedback, request change. Each scene polish. Multiple pass: rough mix → temp mix → final mix → notes pass → printmaster. Quality control: technical check (level meter, true peak, dialnorm spec). Multiple deliverable: theatrical (Atmos + 5.1), TV/streaming (5.1 + stereo), international stem (M&E for dub language). Approval + sign-off: Director + Producer + studio.

Mixing console + signal flow

Console: Avid S6 (industry standard), Harrison MPC4-D, SSL System T. Signal flow expert. Routing complex multi-stem. Read: "The Art of Mixing" (Gibson).

Acoustic + psychoacoustic ear

Trained ear over decade. Hear subtle change. Hiểu psychoacoustic (masking, Fletcher-Munson curve, spatial cue). Reference monitor know.

Storytelling + film sense

Film grammar literacy. Story arc. Emotional rhythm. Sound design support narrative. Watch + study film obsessively.

Pro Tools + Atmos tool

Pro Tools HDX industry standard. Dolby Atmos Renderer. Nuendo alternative. Plugin: FabFilter, Waves, Plugin Alliance, iZotope RX, Sound Particles.

Technical standard + spec

Loudness standard: EBU R128 (TV), CALM Act (US TV), Dolby + DTS theatrical. True peak limit. Dialnorm. Atmos delivery spec. Netflix + Disney + Amazon spec specific.

Director collaboration

Listen carefully. Translate vague creative direction to technical action ("warmer", "tighter", "more space"). Patient + responsive. Decade-long relationship với favorite director.
