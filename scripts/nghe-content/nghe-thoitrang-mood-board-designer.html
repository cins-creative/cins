01Mood Board Designer là ai?

Mood Board Designer trong fashion là chuyên gia tạo visual reference board để define creative direction. Thực ra, mood board thường được làm bởi nhiều role khác nhau (Fashion Designer, Stylist, Creative Director, Trend Forecaster). Là "specialized skill within multiple role" hơn là pure job title. Phân loại theo mục đích: (1) Collection mood board — cho design team start collection; (2) Campaign mood board — cho photoshoot direction; (3) Brand mood board — overarching brand identity; (4) Trend forecasting mood board — for trend agency (WGSN, Trendstop); (5) Lookbook + editorial mood board. Phân loại theo medium: Physical board (corkboard, fabric swatch — traditional), Digital board (Pinterest, Figma, InDesign).

Khác với Trend Forecaster (dự đoán xu hướng) — Mood Board Designer apply trend + reference into actionable visual brief. Khác với Visual Merchandiser (store display) — Mood Board upstream creative phase. Khác với Fashion Stylist (style on model) — Mood Board pre-styling reference. Hợp tác chặt với: Creative Director, Fashion Designer, Stylist, Photographer, Marketing, Trend Forecaster.

Mood Board task thường early in creative process — first 1-3 tuần của collection / campaign cycle. Worked by Fashion Designer, Stylist, Creative Director hoặc dedicated junior trong agency / brand. 02Công việc của một Mood Board Designer

1. Receive brief + research

Receive brief từ Creative Director / Designer: theme cho collection ("Brutalist Romanticism", "Post-apocalyptic Couture", "Cong Tri SS25 — Ao Dai meets New York street"), target customer, season, brand identity. Research: art history, architecture, film, music, current cultural moment, sub-culture, street style. Source from magazine archive, museum visit, Pinterest, Instagram, trend agency report (WGSN, Trendstop).

  • Brief understand
  • Research broad
  • Source reference

2. Curate + select hi-impact image

From 100s of reference, curate down to 20-50 hi-impact. Each image must say something specific — not just pretty. Mix: fashion image (runway, editorial, vintage), non-fashion (art, architecture, nature, abstract), texture / material swatch (fabric photo, surface close-up), color story (palette extraction). Group by sub-theme: silhouette, texture, color, attitude.

  • Curate hi-impact
  • Mix variety
  • Group by theme

3. Build board — physical hoặc digital

Physical mood board (traditional, still preferred high-end): print photo, pin on corkboard / foam board, attach fabric swatch, color paint chip, ribbon, found object. Arrange compositionally. Edit ruthless — remove redundant. Photograph for digital share.

Digital mood board (modern standard): build trong InDesign / Figma / Photoshop / Milanote / Pinterest. Cleaner, easier share, color picker built-in. Both have place — digital for distributed team, physical for in-studio collaboration.

4. Present + iterate + handoff

Present to Creative Director + Designer team. Tell story — không chỉ show image, narrate the why + connection. Receive feedback: "more rebellious", "less sweet", "stronger color saturation". Iterate. Once approved: handoff to designer team — they reference for sketch + sample. Mood board exist physically in studio throughout collection cycle.

03Mood Board Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Visual literacy + curation
📚 Cultural + art history
📋 Composition + design layout
🛠️ Tool: InDesign, Photoshop, Figma, Milanote, Pinterest
💬 Storytelling + presentation
⚡ Trend awareness
Visual literacy + curation

Foundation. Massive visual memory + reference library. Constantly consume image (Vogue archive, Instagram, magazine, museum). Develop "taste" — recognize quality + meaning. Curate edit ruthless — quality > quantity.

Cultural + art history

Mood board reference often deep cultural — Bauhaus, Memphis, Baroque, Japanese wabi-sabi, Vietnamese vintage. Study art history, design history. Watch documentary (BBC Fashion documentary, "Dior and I", "Notebook on Cities and Clothes" Wim Wenders).

Composition + design layout

Mood board self = visual composition. Grid, contrast, hierarchy, focal point, white space. Design fundamental apply.

Tool: InDesign, Photoshop, Figma, Milanote, Pinterest

Adobe InDesign + Photoshop for polished digital. Figma + FigJam for collaborative. Milanote — purpose-built mood board tool. Pinterest for collecting + sharing. Notion for organizing reference library.

Storytelling + presentation

Mood board không speak alone — designer present + narrate. Build narrative arc through image sequence. Verbal articulation. Public speaking trong design review.

Trend awareness

Stay current: WGSN, Vogue Runway, Business of Fashion, ShowStudio, Highsnobiety. Sub-culture awareness — what's bubbling on TikTok, what indie designer doing. Cultural sensitivity — what's appropriating vs honoring.

Học Fashion / Communication / Visual Studies 3–4 năm

Background: Fashion Design / Fashion Communication / Visual Studies / Art History. VN: ĐH Mỹ thuật Công nghiệp Thời trang, RMIT Fashion, FACE Fashion Design. Quốc tế: Central Saint Martins Fashion Communication, Parsons, FIT, Royal College of Art. Plus: massive consumption of fashion magazine + museum visit từ young.

2
Junior at fashion brand / agency / magazine 2-3 năm

Mood board not standalone job — work as Junior Designer, Stylist Assistant, Editorial Assistant, Trend Researcher. VN: Cong Tri Studio, Thuy Design House, Lê Thanh Hòa, ELLE Vietnam, L'Officiel VN, Harper's Bazaar VN. International trend agency: WGSN, Trendstop, Edited.

3
Specialize as Stylist / Visual researcher / Creative associate

3-6 năm — own creative direction work. Specialize: Fashion Stylist (mood board for styling), Visual Researcher (trend agency role), Creative Associate at agency.

4
Senior Stylist / Art Director

6-12 năm — Senior role. Lead creative direction. Mentor junior. Pitch to client.

5
Creative Director

12+ năm — Creative Director at fashion brand. Define seasonal direction. Mood board only one of multiple responsibility.

6
Sự nghiệp Mood Board Designer

Long-term path: (1) Creative Director at major fashion brand; (2) Stylist independent — high-end editorial + brand client; (3) Trend Forecaster at WGSN / Trendstop — niche valuable role; (4) Fashion Editor at magazine — Vogue, ELLE; (5) Brand Consultant — work multiple client; (6) Independent brand founder.
