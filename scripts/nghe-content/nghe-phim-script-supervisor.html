Trên set "The Avengers", actor mặc áo nâu trong scene 1, áo xanh trong scene 2, mì uống cà phê tay trái trong shot A, tay phải trong shot B → khi cut → audience giật mình. Sự khác biệt giữa pro film + amateur = Script Supervisor (Scripty).

Script Supervisor phim (informal: Scripty) là continuity guardian trên set — track every shot, take, prop position, costume, dialogue change. Bảo đảm phim cut smooth + no continuity error. Critical role bridge giữa Director vision + final cut reality. Without Scripty, post-production nightmare + audience-noticeable error.

01Script Supervisor phim là ai?

Script Supervisor là chuyên gia continuity + on-set script management. Phân loại theo project: (1) Feature film Scripty — 4-12 tuần shoot; (2) TV series Scripty — episodic, faster pace; (3) Commercial Scripty — 1-5 ngày; (4) Documentary Scripty — more loose, focus on log. Phân loại theo experience: (1) Junior Scripty — small project; (2) Mid Scripty — TV episode + small feature; (3) Senior Scripty — major feature, complex production; (4) Showrunner-trusted Scripty — long-term TV partnership (Game of Thrones, Breaking Bad continuity).

Khác với Screenwriter (write script pre-production) — Scripty execute script on set. Khác với 1st AD (run-the-set) — Scripty continuity specific. Khác với Production Coordinator (office logistics) — Scripty creative continuity. Khác với Picture Editor (post-production) — Scripty pre-cut continuity to enable smooth cut. Hợp tác chặt với: Director (primary — Scripty is Director's right-hand), 1st AD, DP + Camera dept, Picture Editor (downstream user), Wardrobe + MU + Hair (continuity check), Actor (dialogue check).

02Công việc của một Script Supervisor phim

1. Pre-production prep

Script breakdown: detail per scene — prop, costume, action, dialogue. One-liner: scene summary table. Script timing: estimate page-to-screen-time (1 page = 1 minute approx) → estimate total runtime. Continuity research: previous scene référence, period accuracy. Director meeting: align on Director vision.

03Script Supervisor phim cần giỏi gì?

🔍 Extreme attention to detail
📋 Organization + multi-track
🎬 Film grammar + continuity
📝 Note + document
💬 Communication + diplomacy
⚡ Fast + composed under pressure
Extreme attention to detail

Notice every detail — actor pose, prop position, wardrobe button, hair part, light direction. Photographic memory helps. Read: "The Continuity Supervisor" (Avril Rowlands) — bible of Scripty.

Organization + multi-track

Hundreds of detail per shoot day. Multiple track parallel. Tool: ScriptE Continuity (specialist software), Final Draft, iScript Notes, StudioBinder. Plus physical binder + paper backup.

Film grammar + continuity

Hiểu deep film grammar — eyeline, screen direction, 180-degree rule, cut on action, match cut. Know when violate intentional vs error. Read film theory + watch obsessively.

Note + document

Clear concise note. Editor can read year later understand. Industry-standard format. Photography of every setup. Backup digital + physical.

Communication + diplomacy

Whisper to Director discreetly about continuity issue. Communicate respectfully với actor. Diplomatic when push back on Director suggestion (script timing concern, continuity issue).

Fast + composed under pressure

Set move fast. No "wait, let me check". Decisive note in real-time. Calm under pressure when problem arise.
