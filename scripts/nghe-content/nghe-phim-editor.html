Đạo diễn shoot 100 giờ footage cho phim 2 tiếng. Trong phòng tối, một người ngồi trước Avid và quyết định: take 3 của shot 47, hold thêm 12 frame, cắt sang reaction shot, dissolve sang flashback. Đó là Editor — người mà Walter Murch gọi là "co-author kín tiếng nhất" của phim.

Khác với Đạo diễn (lead vision on set) — Editor lead vision trong phòng dựng. Phim thật sự ra đời trong editing, không phải trên set. Hai pair iconic: Scorsese + Thelma Schoonmaker (50 năm), Spielberg + Michael Kahn — Editor là partner suốt sự nghiệp.

01Editor phim là ai?

Editor phim là người chọn take, cắt rhythm, build pacing và emotional arc của phim từ raw footage. Họ work với hundreds of hour material — multiple take per shot, multiple angle, multiple option. Decide: which take (performance, technical quality), where to cut (point of cut critical), transition (cut, dissolve, fade, match cut), pacing (scene rhythm, scene length), structure (sometimes reorder scene, cut subplot). Final cut là gần như "rewrite" phim trong phòng tối.

Khác với Editor animation (work với animatic + scheduled animation) — Editor live-action work với raw footage có sẵn. Khác với Assembly Editor (first pass — simply put scenes in order) — Editor refine sâu. Khác với Sound Editor (focus audio post) — Editor picture cut. Khác với Colorist (color grade) — Editor structure + rhythm. Có sub-specialty: Feature Film Editor, TV / Streaming Editor, Documentary Editor, Trailer Editor (marketing trailer cut).

  • Chọn take từ multiple option per shot
  • Cut rhythm — point of cut, pacing
  • Build emotional arc qua structure
  • Final cut với sound, music, color integration

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Pre-production read script + meet Director. Total 6 tháng-1.5 năm per feature.

02Công việc của một Editor phim

1. Ingest & assembly cut

During shoot (production phase) — daily Editor ingest footage từ set, sync with sound. Assembly cut: put best take của mỗi shot in script order, raw cut. Editor work parallel với shoot — provide rough scene to Director sau few day. Editor cũng spot issue: missing coverage, performance problem, continuity error — flag cho Director rời set quay supplemental.

  • Daily ingest + sync
  • Assembly cut — best take per shot
  • Issue flagging — coverage problem

03Editor phim cần giỏi gì?

🎵 Sense về rhythm & cut point

💬 Collaborate Director — trust deep
🎨 Visual taste & framing

Sense về rhythm & cut point

Editing essence: where to cut. Walter Murch's 6 priorities of cut: emotion (51%), story (23%), rhythm (10%), eye-trace (7%), 2D plane of screen (5%), 3D space (4%). Cut on emotion above all. Practice via: re-cut favorite scene multiple way, A/B compare. Read "In the Blink of an Eye" (Walter Murch) — Bible of editing.

Watch documentary "The Cutting Edge: The Magic of Movie Editing" (2004) — interview top editor.

Storytelling — restructure scene

Editor là last rewriter. Phim trên page khác phim trên screen — pacing, rhythm, audience reaction change everything. Editor must sense weak point, cut subplot, reorder scene. Famous: Annie Hall original 2.5 tiếng + drama; Editor + Woody Allen restructure thành 90 phút romantic comedy — won Best Picture.

Avid Media Composer mastery

Industry standard cho feature. Avid Media Composer — master deep (multi-cam edit, multi-version, complex bin, keyboard fluent). Premiere Pro common indie / streaming. DaVinci Resolve growing. Take Avid course (Avid Certified User), practice via short project.

Collaborate Director — trust deep

Editor-Director relationship intimate — sit together 6+ tiếng/ngày for months. Build trust: deliver good cut consistently, respect vision, suggest without ego, listen. Best pair work multi-decade: Schoonmaker+Scorsese, Kahn+Spielberg, Cassidy+Linklater. Career Editor build through repeat collaboration.

Visual taste & framing

Editor choose take based on framing, performance, light. Phải có visual taste sharp. Study cinematography parallel với editing — biết DP intent giúp pick right take. Watch film analytically, identify why specific cut work.

Stamina & patience

Edit session 8–12 tiếng. Detail-obsessed work — adjust 2-frame cuts. Live với same phim for months. Stay creative throughout. Pace energy. Take walk break. Manage burnout.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Editor phim?

1
Học film / editing 4–5 năm

VN: ĐH Sân khấu Điện ảnh (Dựng phim Hà Nội + TP.HCM). Quốc tế top: AFI Conservatory (Editing strongest program), USC, NYU Tisch, NFTS (UK), La Fémis Paris. Foundation: film history, editing principle, sound, narrative theory. Critical: edit own short extensively during school.

2
Master Avid + Premiere — 1000+ hour

Tool mastery foundation. Avid certification, Premiere Adobe certified. Free: YouTube "InsideTheEdit", "This Guy Edits". Practice via own short, friend's short, re-cut existing film footage. Buy small home setup (Mac + Avid subscription).

3
Entry: Editorial Assistant / 2nd AE

Path entry chính: Editorial Assistant (EA) hoặc 2nd Assistant Editor trên feature. Handle media management, sync, prep. 2–4 năm EA học inside editing room. VN: Galaxy Studio, BHD post, Tuấn Studio. International: Major studio post house.

4
1st Assistant Editor

Promote thành 1st AE — sometimes cut scene independent, manage editorial pipeline. Build trust với Editor. 2–4 năm 1st AE typical before promote Editor. Cross-cut TV episode is fastest path.

5
Editor solo — TV episode → indie feature

First editor credit usually: TV episode (drama series, sitcom episode rotate editor), indie low-budget feature, documentary (faster path for Editor — easier to step up cut). Build credit. Reel quan trọng — cut piece show emotional moment + technical skill.

6
Sự nghiệp Editor

Long-term: (1) Established Feature Editor — work với top Director repeatedly, prestige + decent rate; (2) Trailer Editor — high rate, faster project (advertising-style); (3) TV Lead Editor — series consistency (Game of Thrones, Succession); (4) Documentary Editor specialist; (5) Cross to Director — Editor experience valuable (Asghar Farhadi). Lương: VN entry 15–25M, mid 30–60M, feature editor 50–120M; US Editor $80K–$300K, A-list (working với Spielberg, Scorsese) $500K–$1M+/film. Career often 30–50 năm — Thelma Schoonmaker editing into her 80s.
