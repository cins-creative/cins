Kết hợp aesthetic, brand strategy, function (protect product), regulatory (compliance), sustainability. Là role specialty cao của graphic design industry.

01Packaging Designer là ai?

Packaging Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế toàn bộ packaging system của sản phẩm — primary container (bottle, box), label, secondary box, shipper, point-of-sale display. Phân loại theo category: (1) FMCG / CPG Packaging Designer — food, beverage, household; (2) Beauty + cosmetic Packaging Designer — premium structural design; (3) Electronics / tech Packaging Designer — Apple-level unboxing experience; (4) Pharmaceutical Packaging Designer — regulatory complex; (5) Luxury Packaging Designer — fashion accessory, jewelry; (6) Sustainable / eco Packaging Designer — biodegradable, recycled, refill model. Phân loại theo company: Design agency (Pearlfisher, JKR, Pentagram), Brand in-house (Apple, P&G, Unilever, L'Oreal), Structural packaging studio.

Khác với Graphic Designer general — Packaging focus 3D physical + production constraint. Khác với Industrial Designer (product itself) — Packaging wrapper. Khác với Brand Designer (identity system) — Packaging apply identity to package medium. Khác với Print Designer (flat print) — Packaging structural + dimensional. Hợp tác chặt với: Brand Manager, Marketing Director, Industrial Designer, Print production, Manufacturer + supplier, Regulatory + legal team, Sustainability team.

03Packaging Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Graphic design foundation
📦 Structural + dieline understanding
🛠️ Tool: Illustrator, Photoshop, KeyShot
📋 Material + production knowledge
📈 Brand strategy + marketing
🌱 Sustainability + regulatory
Graphic design foundation

Foundation. Typography mastery, color, layout, hierarchy, photography direction. Strong fundamentals = beautiful packaging. Study iconic: Pentagram, JKR, Pearlfisher, Turner Duckworth, Landor, Sagmeister Walsh work.

Structural + dieline understanding

Hiểu 3D structural — how flat dieline become folded box. Geometry + math. Material behavior. Work với structural packaging specialist when complex. Study dieline library, manufacturing process.

Tool: Illustrator, Photoshop, KeyShot

Illustrator — primary label + dieline tool. Photoshop — image + photo manipulation. InDesign — multi-page packaging document. KeyShot / Cinema 4D / Blender — 3D mockup. Esko ArtiosCAD — structural packaging professional tool.

Material + production knowledge

Hiểu material: paperboard (chipboard, kraft, SBS), corrugated, plastic (PET, HDPE, glass), foil, tin. Print process: offset, digital, flexographic, gravure. Finishing: foil, emboss, UV, soft-touch lamination, varnish. Visit printer + manufacturer regular.

Brand strategy + marketing

Packaging serve brand + marketing. Hiểu brand architecture, positioning, target audience, channel strategy. Read Marty Neumeier brand book.

Sustainability + regulatory

Sustainability hot topic — recycled, biodegradable, refill, mono-material. Regulatory: nutrition label format, ingredient list, warning, country-specific compliance (FDA, EU, Vietnam VFA). Stay updated.
