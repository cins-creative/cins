01Production Coordinator phim là ai?

Production Coordinator (PC) là chuyên gia operational coordination trong film production. Phân loại theo phase: (1) Pre-production PC — heavy paperwork, crew hire, vendor setup; (2) Production PC — daily logistics during shoot; (3) Post-production PC — coordinate edit, VFX, sound vendor. Phân loại theo level: (1) Office PC — primary office-based; (2) Travel PC — location shoot, set up production office in new city; (3) Senior PC — lead PC team; (4) Production Manager (next level). Phân loại theo project: Feature film, TV series, Commercial, Music video.

Khác với Producer (overall lead) — Coordinator operational. Khác với Line Producer (budget + day-to-day) — Coordinator under Line Producer, more granular. Khác với Production Assistant (entry) — Coordinator mid-level office, supervise PA. Khác với 1st AD (set-focused) — Coordinator office-focused. Hợp tác chặt với: Line Producer + Production Manager, Department head (DP, PD, Costume, Locations), 1st AD + 2nd AD, PA team, Vendor + casting, Transport + crafty.

  • Logistics + schedule coordinate
  • Crew + vendor contract + paperwork
  • Communication hub between team
  • Call sheet + script revision distribute

02Công việc của một Production Coordinator phim

1. Pre-production setup + crew hire

Setup production office: physical space, equipment, software (Movie Magic, StudioBinder, ShotGrid). Crew hire paperwork: contract per crew member, NDA, deal memo, W-9 / tax form. Vendor setup: equipment rental, transport, catering, hotel block. Insurance: production insurance, workers comp, completion bond. Location agreement coordinate với Locations Department.

  • Office setup
  • Crew contract
  • Vendor + insurance

03Production Coordinator phim cần giỏi gì?

📋 Organization + multi-tasking
🛠️ Movie Magic / StudioBinder
💬 Communication + diplomacy
🎬 Film production knowledge
⚖️ Contract + legal basics

Organization + multi-tasking

Track everyone, everything. System critical — to-do list, calendar, file system, naming convention. Detail obsession. Nothing slip.

Movie Magic / StudioBinder

Movie Magic Budgeting + Scheduling — industry standard. StudioBinder — modern alternative. Final Draft — script. Microsoft Office / Google Workspace. Slack + Email. QuickBooks / accounting tool. Master multiple tool.

Communication + diplomacy

Hub. Communicate với Director, Producer, Star, crew, vendor, lawyer — different style each. Diplomacy. Manage difficult personality. Clear concise message. Documentation paper trail.

Film production knowledge

Hiểu production workflow + crew structure + department function. Terminology. Without this, scheduling unrealistic + coordination fail.

Contract + legal basics

Handle contract daily. Hiểu basic terms — crew deal memo, vendor agreement, location release, talent contract. Cannot replace lawyer but recognize issue. Read: "This Business of Film".

Calm crisis management

Emergency happens daily. Stay calm. Solve fast. Don't panic. Reassure team. Prioritize. Make tough call quick.
