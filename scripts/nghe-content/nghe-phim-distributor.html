Phim "Em và Trịnh" làm xong xuôi — chỉ có công ty phân phối quyết định nó chiếu 50 rạp hay 500 rạp, có lên Netflix sau bao lâu, có ra international không. Phim "Parasite" win Cannes — bởi vì Neon (distributor Mỹ) chiến lược release thông minh, nó win Oscar Best Picture sau đó.

Khác với Producer (làm ra phim) — Distributor đưa phim đến khán giả. Họ là bridge giữa sản xuất và market — quyết định timing, channel, marketing để phim reach right audience right time.

01Distributor (Nhà phân phối phim) là ai?

Distributor là công ty (hoặc người trong công ty đó) đưa phim từ studio đến khán giả. Họ acquire distribution right của phim, sau đó release qua: rạp chiếu (theatrical), streaming (Netflix, Disney+, Apple TV+, VN: FPT Play, Galaxy Play), TV broadcast, DVD/Blu-ray, airline. Mỗi window có deal riêng. Distributor manage: marketing campaign (poster, trailer, ads, PR), release strategy (số rạp, ngày, market), festival circuit (Cannes, TIFF cho prestige), international sale.

Khác với Producer (own production — financing, hiring, shoot) — Distributor own release + marketing. Khác với Cinema Operator (own rạp — CGV, Lotte, BHD) — Distributor cung cấp content cho cinema. Khác với Streaming Platform (Netflix, FPT — tech + content aggregator) — Distributor cung cấp content cho streaming (hoặc một số platform self-distribute). Có sub-specialty: Theatrical Distribution Manager, International Sales Agent, Acquisitions Executive, Festival Strategist.

  • Acquire distribution right từ producer/sales agent
  • Plan release strategy — date, screen count, market
  • Manage marketing campaign — poster, trailer, ads
  • Negotiate với cinema, streaming, TV

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Distributor là company / division, không phải single role. Bên trong gồm: Acquisitions team (scout phim), Theatrical Distribution Manager (rạp), Marketing team (campaign), Publicity / PR (press, talent appearance), International Sales (sell territory), Festival Strategist, Booking/Sales Manager (negotiate với rạp). Phối hợp với Producer / Sales Agent (upstream), Cinema chain / Streaming platform (downstream), Press / Influencer. Tham gia: từ phim wrap hoặc rough cut (8–12 tháng trước release) đến post-release window expire.

02Công việc của một Distributor

1. Acquisition — scout & buy phim

Distributor scout phim qua festival (Cannes Market, Berlin EFM, Toronto, AFM) — đây là marketplace lớn nhất. Acquisition team watch hàng trăm phim per festival, decide bid which. Negotiate distribution right: theatrical only, all rights, specific territory, advance payment + revenue share. Deal có thể $10K (indie nhỏ) đến $50M+ (Sundance hit — CODA Apple TV+).

  • Festival scouting — Cannes, Sundance, TIFF
  • Screening + evaluation
  • Negotiate deal — advance + rev share

2. Release strategy & planning

Sau acquire — plan release. Release date: trùng holiday, summer blockbuster, awards season; tránh blockbuster cạnh tranh. Screen count: wide (3000+ rạp), limited (5–50 rạp prestige), platform release (start nhỏ, expand). Market: domestic vs international, tier-1 city first. Window: theatrical → premium VOD → streaming → TV.

  • Release date pick — avoid competition
  • Screen count strategy
  • Window plan — theatrical → VOD → streaming

3. Marketing campaign

Distributor invest 30–100% production budget vào marketing. Manage: poster design, trailer cut (multiple version — teaser, full, TV spot, social), paid media (TV ads, online, OOH, social), PR campaign (talent press tour, junket, late night show), partnership (brand tie-in, cinema chain promotion), social media, influencer, screening (preview, premiere). Coordinate timing — peak attention week before release.

  • Creative asset — poster, trailer, key art
  • Paid media — TV, online, OOH
  • PR & talent — press tour, junket

4. Booking, sales & reporting

Negotiate với cinema chain (CGV, Lotte, BHD, Galaxy, Beta, internationally AMC, Cinemark, Wanda) — screen count, screen hour, rev share (typically 40-50% rạp, 50-60% distributor first weeks, shift dần). Track box office daily; adjust marketing real-time. Negotiate international sale per territory. Window expiry: move to streaming, TV.

  • Cinema booking — screen, hour
  • Box office tracking
  • International & ancillary sale

03Distributor cần giỏi gì?

💬 Marketing & storytelling commercial

⚡ Risk-taking & conviction
Hiểu market & audience

Distributor predict: phim này sẽ resonate với audience nào, market nào, season nào. Skill này dựa trên: watch nhiều phim (every weekend, every genre, every country), read trade publication (Variety, Hollywood Reporter, Screen Daily, VN: Tuổi Trẻ Văn hoá, Báo Điện ảnh Việt Nam), track box office data (Box Office Mojo, IMDB Pro).

Each market khác: VN Tết season giống Mỹ Christmas; horror dominate Halloween; family film school holiday.

Business & deal making

Distributor business core: contract negotiation. Phải hiểu: territory split, term length, rev share, advance/MG (minimum guarantee), reversion clause, holdback (gap giữa theatrical và streaming). Get MBA / Finance background hoặc learn on-job under senior. Read "The Hollywood Distribution Workbook" (Cones).

Marketing & storytelling commercial

Marketing phim = sell idea trong 30 second trailer. Phải hiểu: target demo, campaign strategy, brand position. Learn modern marketing — social era đã transform industry (TikTok organic critical). Watch top marketing campaign breakdown (Marvel, A24).

Data analytics & tracking

Distribution data-driven: box office tracking real-time, awareness survey, screening test, audience exit poll. Phải hiểu data — Comscore, Screen Engine. Spread analysis (premiere weekend vs leg-out vs international ratio). Excel + business intelligence tool.

Taste & phim sense

Acquisitions side đặc biệt — phải spot hit từ rough cut. A24 famous với taste — pick film unconventional rồi turn thành hit (Everything Everywhere All At Once, Moonlight, Lady Bird). Develop taste qua: watch independent + festival film extensively.

Risk-taking & conviction

Distribution risky business — invest millions upfront, hit-or-miss. Conviction về phim quan trọng — defend trong meeting, scale marketing khi early signal positive. Acceptance loss — most phim không profitable; phải pick winner balance loser.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Distributor?

1
Học film business / marketing / law

Background phù hợp: Film Studies + Business, Marketing, Communication, Law (entertainment law). VN: ĐH KHXH&NV (Báo chí, Điện ảnh), ĐH Kinh tế (Marketing), ĐH Luật. Quốc tế: USC School of Cinematic Arts (Film Industry program), UCLA Anderson MBA-Film. Plus: watch hàng nghìn phim — foundation taste + market sense.

2
Entry: intern / assistant tại distributor

Path entry: Marketing Assistant, Acquisitions Coordinator, Sales Assistant tại distributor. VN: Galaxy Distribution, BHD, Mega GS, CGV Distribution, FilmFlame, ProductionQ. International: A24, Neon, IFC Films, Magnolia, Sony Pictures Classics, Lionsgate. Volunteer at film festival (HANIFF, Liên hoan Phim VN, regional film fest) — network.

3
Specialize: Acquisitions / Marketing / Sales

Sau 2–3 năm generalist — pick path: Acquisitions Executive (scout + buy), Marketing Manager (campaign), Sales Manager (cinema booking, international), Publicity Director. Each path khác skill emphasis.

4
Build track record & relationship

Distribution là relationship business. Build với: producer (acquire source), sales agent (international), cinema executive, festival programmer, talent rep (PR access). Năm 5–10 đầu invest network. Track record: phim bạn handle, ROI bạn deliver.

5
Senior role / Head of

Path: VP/Senior Director Acquisitions, Head of Marketing, President of Distribution. At major studio (Warner Bros, Universal, Disney, VN: Galaxy, CGV) — corporate ladder. At indie distributor (A24, Neon) — smaller team, faster impact.

6
Sự nghiệp Distribution

Long-term: (1) Senior exec tại major — corporate stable; (2) Founder own distribution label (Neon, A24 from scratch); (3) Cross to producer side — leverage market sense; (4) Streaming platform exec (Netflix, Disney+ — content acquisition). Lương: VN entry 15–25M, senior 40–80M, VP 100–200M+. International: major studio VP $200K–$500K + bonus; A24 senior $300K–$1M+. Industry transforming với streaming — bringing new opportunity + disruption.
