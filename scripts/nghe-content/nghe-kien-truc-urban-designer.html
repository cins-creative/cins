Khi đi dạo bờ Hồ Tây với plaza, bench, vegetation tạo cảm giác liveable, khi khu phố cổ Hà Nội pedestrian-friendly + mixed-use vibrant, khi Singapore's Marina Bay area trở thành global icon nhờ thoughtful urban design, khi Vinhomes Ocean Park master plan có internal walkable street + canal — đó là tác phẩm của Urban Designer.

Urban Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế không gian đô thị tại scale block + neighborhood + district — street, plaza, park, transit hub, mixed-use development. Bridge giữa Architecture (single building) và Urban Planning (region + policy). Critical cho liveable city + sustainable urbanism. Việt Nam đô thị hóa nhanh → Urban Designer demand explosive.

01Urban Designer là ai?

Urban Designer là chuyên gia design urban form tại middle scale. Phân loại theo scale: (1) Block / street scale — pedestrian, plaza, streetscape; (2) Neighborhood scale — 10-100 hectare master plan; (3) District / city center — CBD, mixed-use; (4) Transit-oriented development — quanh metro station; (5) Waterfront / riverfront — Saigon River, West Lake. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Master plan — large new development; (2) Public realm design — street, plaza, park; (3) Urban regeneration — old district renew; (4) Transit + mobility planning; (5) Resilient urbanism — climate adapt. Phân loại theo career: (1) Junior Urban Designer; (2) Urban Designer; (3) Senior; (4) Principal / Director; (5) Founding Partner. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Urban design firm — Gehl, Sasaki, SOM Urban; (2) Architecture firm với urban arm; (3) Government planning department; (4) Developer in-house — VinGroup, Sun Group, Becamex; (5) Academic + research.

Khác với Urban Planner — Planner focus policy + zoning + region scale, Urban Designer focus physical form + middle scale (overlap exist). Khác với Architect — Architect single building, Urban Designer between buildings + public realm. Khác với Landscape Architect — Landscape focus green + ecology, Urban Designer broader (build + green + circulation). Khác với Real Estate Developer — Developer commercial driver, Urban Designer design output. Hợp tác chặt với: Urban Planner (policy), Architect (building), Landscape Architect, Transportation Engineer, Government official + Planning Commission, Developer + Investor, Community + stakeholder.

02Công việc của một Urban Designer

1. Site analysis + research + program

Foundation. Site visit + observe: existing condition, circulation, view, microclimate. Historical + cultural research: site memory, identity. Demographic + economic analysis: who use, what activity. Regulatory framework: zoning, FAR, height limit, easement. Climate + environmental: sun path, wind, flood risk. Stakeholder mapping: government, developer, community, business.

  • Site analysis + observe
  • Demographic + economic
  • Regulatory + climate

2. Concept + master plan design — primary work

Core. Concept development: design idea, big move. Master plan design: figure-ground (building vs open), circulation network (vehicle + bike + pedestrian + transit), open space system (plaza + park + green corridor), building envelope (massing + height), program distribution (residential + commercial + civic + cultural). Multiple alternative: 2-3 scheme test. 3D model: SketchUp, Rhino, Lumion. Diagram: communicate concept clearly. Cross-section + view study.

03Urban Designer cần giỏi gì?

Urban design theory + history

Vitruvius → Haussmann → Le Corbusier → Jane Jacobs → New Urbanism → Tactical Urbanism. Read: "The Death and Life of Great American Cities" (Jacobs), "A Pattern Language" (Alexander), "Image of the City" (Lynch), "Cities for People" (Gehl), "Walkable City" (Speck).

Architecture + spatial design

Massing, proportion, scale. Building typology. Façade + edge condition. Spatial sequence. Read: "Form, Space, and Order" (Ching).

SketchUp + Rhino + GIS

SketchUp standard urban tool. Rhino + Grasshopper parametric. AutoCAD 2D base. ArcGIS / QGIS data analysis. Lumion + Enscape + Twinmotion render. Photoshop + InDesign presentation.

Planning + policy literacy

Zoning. FAR. Form-based code. Transit-oriented development. Affordable housing policy. Vietnam Luật Quy hoạch + Luật Xây dựng. Read: "Place and Placelessness" (Relph).

Sustainability + climate

Walkability. Mixed-use. Green infrastructure. Climate resilience (sea level, flood, heat). LEED-ND, WELL community. Read: "Resilient City" (Vale + Campanella).

Communication + stakeholder

Diagram + render compellingly. Public workshop facilitate. Government present. Developer negotiate. Community listen. Multi-stakeholder navigate.
