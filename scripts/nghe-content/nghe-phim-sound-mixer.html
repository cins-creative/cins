Sound Mixer phim là kỹ sư âm thanh hiện trường — ghi âm dialogue + ambient on set during shoot. Là head of sound crew on set. Critical role — bad production sound = expensive ADR (Automated Dialogue Replacement) post. Boom + lavalier mic master. Quiet on set! → Sound Mixer's domain.

01Sound Mixer phim là ai?

Sound Mixer (Production Sound Mixer / Location Sound Mixer) là head of sound department on film set. Phân loại theo project: (1) Feature film — Hollywood, indie; (2) TV series + drama — Netflix series; (3) Documentary; (4) Commercial; (5) News + reality TV. Phân loại theo crew size: (1) Solo / 2-man crew — Mixer + Boom Op; (2) Standard 3-person crew — Mixer + Boom Op + Utility; (3) Large crew — multi-Boom Op, multi-Utility, A2/B2. Phân loại theo budget: (1) Indie / low-budget; (2) Mid-budget studio; (3) Blockbuster.

Khác với Sound Designer (post create) — Sound Mixer on-set capture. Khác với Boom Operator (operate boom mic, report to Mixer) — Mixer head. Khác với Re-recording Mixer (post mix) — different phase. Khác với Sound Editor (cut + edit) — different phase. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, Cinematographer / DOP, 1st AD (call sheet + schedule), Boom Operator, Sound Utility / A2, Actor (lavalier placement), Costume (mic hide), Production Sound Mixer Department crew.

Crew hire: Boom Operator + Utility. Schedule coordinate: với 1st AD on shoot day.

3. Record take + ride level

03Sound Mixer phim cần giỏi gì?

⚡ Wireless RF management

🛠️ Troubleshoot fast
📋 Documentation + paperwork
Mic + recorder technical

Master Sound Devices 833/888/Scorpio, Sennheiser MKH series, Schoeps CMIT/CMC, Sanken Cos-11/DPA 6060 lavalier, Lectrosonics / Wisycom / Audio Ltd wireless. Read: "Location Sound Bible" (Yewdall), "The Sound Effects Bible" (Viers).

Critical listening

Hear subtle noise, distortion, frequency issue. Trained ear. Audio quality reference. Studio + field experience build. Hifi headphone (Sony MDR-7506, Sennheiser HD25, DT770).

Wireless RF management

Wireless frequency coordinate. RF scan + frequency assignment. Avoid interference (WiFi, cell, broadcast). License regulation. Tool: Shure Workbench, Wireless Workbench, ARS.

Set etiquette + protocol

Film set protocol. Chain of command. Communication discipline. "Quiet on set" authority. Diplomacy với actor + director + AD. Patience long hour.

Troubleshoot fast

Equipment fail mid-take? RF noise sudden? Battery die? Solve fast under pressure. Backup equipment ready. Common issue knowledge.

Documentation + paperwork

Sound report every take. Slate + timecode log. Equipment rental paper. Insurance paper. Crew schedule. Tool: Movie Slate, Sound Devices SD-Remote.
