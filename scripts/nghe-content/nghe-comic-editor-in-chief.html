01Editor-in-Chief comic là ai?

Editor-in-Chief (EIC) comic là người lead vision biên tập của comic publisher. Họ own: portfolio strategy (genre balance, target audience, brand position), talent acquisition (sign top author, retain), quality bar (mỗi series approve hoặc reject), editorial team leadership (manage Editor under), industry relationship (với platform, agent, IP licensor), annual planning (release calendar, expansion category). Ở scale lớn (Shueisha, Marvel, Naver Webtoon) — EIC là job có influence vast trong industry.

Khác với Editor (focus per-series biên tập) — EIC scope toàn catalog. Khác với Publisher / CEO (focus business toàn diện — finance, ops) — EIC focus creative direction. Khác với Editorial Director (manage editor team, slightly lower than EIC) — EIC top of editorial. Có sub-specialty: EIC mainstream (Marvel, DC), EIC seinen/josei (mature manga), EIC webtoon platform.

  • Define portfolio strategy — genre, audience
  • Sign top author / artist talent
  • Set quality bar — approve/reject series
  • Lead editor team dưới

Editor-in-Chief ở top of editorial layer. Báo cáo cho Publisher / CEO hoặc Board. Tham gia: portfolio annual planning, weekly editorial review, talent meeting recurring, industry event/award.

02Công việc của một Editor-in-Chief comic

1. Portfolio strategy & planning

Annual + recurring strategy work. Portfolio analysis: genre balance (shōnen action, romance, slice-of-life, isekai, horror), audience segment (teen vs adult, male vs female), market trend (isekai trend, BL/GL rising). Plan release calendar: launch X new series per quarter, retire underperformer, double-down hit. Make strategic bet — new genre, new format, new IP investment.

  • Genre portfolio audit
  • Annual release calendar
  • Strategic bet — new genre/format

Vision & strategic thinking

EIC nghĩ 2–5 năm forward: genre nào trend, format nào emerge, brand publisher đứng đâu. Skill này build qua nhiều năm industry experience + extensive consumption + business acumen. Study competitor (Shueisha vs Kodansha vs Shogakukan vs Webtoon Korea), analyze pattern of hit.

Read business strategy + entertainment industry book. Watch industry talk (BoF, Comic-Con panel).

Hit picking — instinct về thị trường

EIC must spot hit potential từ pitch. Marvel's Stan Lee famous với spotting Spider-Man, X-Men (initially rejected by other editor). Shueisha's editor (greenlight One Piece despite atypical pitch). Build instinct qua: đọc thousand pitch, track which become hit, identify pattern (relatable protagonist, fresh hook, sustainable concept).

Leadership editor team

EIC manage 5–30 editor under (multi-level). Develop: hiring sense (find right editor for genre), mentoring, conflict resolution, performance management. Build editorial culture — quality bar, ritual, mentorship pairing. Read management book ("Radical Candor", "Multipliers").

Network & deal making

Industry là relationship business. EIC build network: top author, agent, platform exec, IP licensor, competitor (frenemies). Negotiation skill: contract clause, IP right, royalty, exclusivity. Long-term relationship trump short-term win.

Business acumen

EIC sit ở table với CEO + board. Phải hiểu: P&L of publisher, advance vs royalty trade-off, IP licensing economic, international rights value, digital vs print economic. Take business course nếu background not strong; partner closely với CFO.

Editorial sense — story + art

EIC maintain editorial chops dù không edit daily. Phải critique pitch sharp, identify story weakness, art quality. Path tới EIC always qua Editor experience — 10–20 năm editor before EIC. Maintain through: weekly editorial review, read all greenlight pitch.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Editor-in-Chief comic?

1
Học nền tảng + đọc cực kỳ nhiều comic

Background phù hợp: Văn học, Báo chí, Mỹ thuật, Marketing. VN: ĐH KHXH&NV, ĐH Báo chí Tuyên truyền, ĐH Mỹ thuật. Quan trọng nhất: đọc thousand series qua nhiều genre, region, era. Build pattern recognition deep.

2
5–10 năm Editor

Path: Editorial Assistant → Editor → Senior Editor → Editorial Director. Trải qua nhiều genre, nhiều series. Build credit qua hit. Master editorial process: pitch evaluation, story development, art direction, talent management. Top publisher VN: Comicola, Skybooks, Phan Thị, NXB Trẻ comic line.

3
Editorial Director (manage editor)

Promote thành Editorial Director — manage Editor team, set department strategy. 2–5 năm trong role này. Develop people management + strategic thinking. Build relationship với CEO + business side.

4
Build industry profile

EIC = public figure. Build profile: speak at industry event, mentor new editor visibly, publish editorial column / interview, judge competition. Visibility opens door cho EIC opportunity.

5
Editor-in-Chief role

Path tới EIC: (1) Internal promotion (most common — Editorial Director promote up); (2) Lateral hire từ competitor (rare, high-profile); (3) Founder own publisher (set yourself as EIC of own company). Usually 10–20 năm in industry trước EIC role.

6
Sự nghiệp EIC

Path long-term: (1) Long tenure EIC (Stan Lee 50 năm tại Marvel; Yamamoto at Shueisha decades); (2) Move to Publisher / CEO; (3) Founder own publishing label; (4) Move to platform exec (Webtoon, Tapas). Lương: VN EIC 80–250M/tháng + bonus tied to portfolio performance; Japan Shueisha EIC ¥30M–¥80M/năm; US Marvel/DC EIC $200K–$500K + bonus. Top influence role trong comic industry — Stan Lee's legacy still shapes Marvel decades later.
