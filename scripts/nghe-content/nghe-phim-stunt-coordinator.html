Khi John Wick có gun-fu choreography mỗi scene precise + cinematic, khi Mission Impossible Tom Cruise hang off Burj Khalifa real safely, khi Mad Max Fury Road's vehicle chaos pulled off without major injury, khi Bond's signature fight sequence have lethal grace — đó là tác phẩm của Stunt Coordinator.

Stunt Coordinator là chuyên gia design + execute stunt sequence — fight, fall, car crash, fire, high fall. Manage stunt team safety. Bond film, John Wick, Mission Impossible iconic action đều có Stunt Coordinator. Safety + spectacle balance — must look dangerous, must be safe. One mistake = serious injury or death.

01Stunt Coordinator phim là ai?

Stunt Coordinator là head of stunt department on film. Phân loại theo career: (1) Stunt Performer — perform stunt; (2) Stunt Coordinator — plan + lead stunt team; (3) Second Unit Director / Stunt Director — direct action sequence; (4) Fight Choreographer / Action Designer — specialty within. Phân loại theo specialty: (1) Fight + martial art — Hong Kong action tradition; (2) Vehicle + driving; (3) High fall + rigging; (4) Fire + pyrotechnic; (5) Water + diving; (6) Horse + equestrian; (7) Aerial + wirework. Phân loại theo project: (1) Action blockbuster; (2) Period film; (3) TV series; (4) Commercial / music video.

Khác với Action Director / Second Unit Director (direct action sequence) — Stunt Coordinator focus on physical + safety, may overlap. Khác với Fight Choreographer (fight specialty) — Stunt Coordinator broader. Khác với Special Effects Coordinator (pyro, prop) — overlap during effect-heavy stunt. Khác với Stunt Double (perform for actor) — Stunt Coordinator manage. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, 1st AD, Stunt team + performer, Actor (rehearse + double), Cinematographer / DOP, Camera operator, Special Effects Coordinator, Rigging Gaffer, Safety officer + medical.

  • Stunt sequence design + plan
  • Stunt team lead + safety
  • Choreography + rehearse
  • Execute + adjust on set

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Stunt Coordinator hoạt động pre-production + production. Báo cáo Director + Producer. Per project: 3-12 tháng (pre-rehearsal heavy). On-set every action day. Stunt team 5-30+ performer depending scope. Physical role still demanding (often Coordinator perform too).

02Công việc của một Stunt Coordinator phim

1. Script breakdown + design + plan

Script breakdown: identify every stunt — fight, fall, vehicle, fire, water. Director meeting: align vision (martial art style? brutal vs elegant? practical vs effect-driven?). Reference + research: classic action film, similar genre, specific martial art tradition. Stunt design: sequence design — beat-by-beat. Equipment plan: rigging, padding, vehicle, weapon, fire effect. Cast doubling plan: who can do what, who need full double. Budget estimate: per sequence.

  • Script + design
  • Director vision
  • Equipment + plan

2. Pre-vis + rehearsal + cast train

Stunt pre-vis: 3D pre-vis hoặc rough action mock. Stunt team hire: per specialty needed. Rehearsal: pre-shoot rehearsal at warehouse / stunt rehearsal space. Choreograph fight beat. 3. On set + execute + safety

Core. Safety briefing pre-shoot: every crew member aware. Equipment setup: rigging, padding, fire safe, vehicle safe. Walk-through: with stunt team + actor + camera + Director — slow-motion run-through. Tự execute: full speed take. Safety officer + medical standby: required. Multiple take: nhiều take needed for coverage. Adjust per take: refine choreography, camera angle. Stop if unsafe: Stunt Coordinator authority to stop if conditions unsafe.

03Stunt Coordinator phim cần giỏi gì?

🥋 Physical + martial art
🛡️ Safety + risk management
🎬 Cinema + camera literacy
🤝 Team leadership
⚙️ Rigging + equipment
💪 Physical stamina
Physical + martial art

Multi-discipline — martial art (Karate, Judo, BJJ, Wushu, MMA), gymnastics, parkour, fall technique. Most Stunt Coordinator have 10-20+ year physical practice. Read: "The Stuntman" (Roberts), study Jackie Chan + Donnie Yen + Yuen Woo-ping work.

Safety + risk management

Safety protocol mastery. Risk assessment. Insurance + liability literacy. Emergency response. WorkSafe regulation. Brandon Lee (Crow accident) lesson — Alec Baldwin Rust incident.

Cinema + camera literacy

Hiểu camera angle, lens, shutter speed, frame rate. Stunt for camera (different than live performance). Read: "The Filmmaker's Eye" (Mercado), "Cinematography: Theory and Practice" (Brown).

Team leadership

Lead stunt team — diverse specialty. Conflict resolution. Diplomacy với actor + director. Decision authority. Discretion.

Rigging + equipment

Wire harness, decelerator, airbag, fall pad, fire suit. Vehicle rig, ramp. Pyro basic. Equipment maintain + inspect.

Physical stamina

Long shoot day. Often perform self. Endurance + recovery. Injury management. Career span limited by body (some transition to Coordinator-only as body age).

Per-project work. Insurance high (high risk profession). Career physical demanding, transition to direction common.
