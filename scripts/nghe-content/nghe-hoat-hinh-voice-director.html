02Công việc của một Voice Director hoạt hình

1. Casting + audition

Foundation. Script read + character analyze: voice + personality match. Casting brief với Director: who lead role, who supporting. Audition organize: self-tape + in-person. Voice match cho ensemble: character voice không sound similar. Star actor consider: A-list actor for marketing. Final cast với Director approval. Contract + schedule: với agent.

2. Recording session direct — primary work

Core. Recording booth setup: với Sound Engineer. Actor warm up + brief: scene context, character emotion. Multiple take direct: variant per emotion, pace, intensity — capture range. 03Voice Director hoạt hình cần giỏi gì?

👂 Performance ear
🎭 Acting + theater literacy
💬 Communication + direction
🎬 Animation literacy
🎙️ Audio + recording technical
📜 Script + character analysis
Performance ear

Subtle nuance hear. Authentic vs forced. Energy level read. Multiple take compare. Read: "Acting in Animation" (Hooks), "The Voice Actor's Workbook" (Apple).

Acting + theater literacy

Acting training. Theater + improv background. Read: "An Actor Prepares" (Stanislavski), "Truth in Comedy" (Halpern + Close + Johnson), "Improvisation for the Theater" (Spolin).

Communication + direction

Note clear + actionable. Adjective rich vocabulary. Encourage + critique balance. Read: "Directing Actors" (Weston), "On Directing Film" (Mamet).

Animation literacy

Hiểu animation principle. Lip-sync requirement. Character design influence voice. Read: "The Animator's Survival Kit" (Williams).

Audio + recording technical

Pro Tools basic. Mic technique. Studio acoustic. Talkback. Coordinate với Sound Engineer.

Script + character analysis

Read script deeply. Character arc. Subtext. Backstory invent. Read: "Story" (McKee), "Save the Cat!" (Snyder).
