01Runner hoạt hình là ai?

Runner là entry-level support staff trong animation studio. Phân loại theo team support: (1) Production Runner — support Producer + Coordinator team; (2) Department Runner — support specific department (Animation, Lighting, FX); (3) Executive Runner — support executive (CEO, COO, VP); (4) Studio Runner — general studio operation; (5) Floor Runner — physical floor errand. Phân loại theo studio: (1) Major animation studio (Pixar, Disney, DreamWorks) — formal Runner program; (2) TV animation studio (Sun Wolf, Nickelodeon) — Runner role; (3) Indie studio — fewer Runner, multi-hat; (4) Stop-motion studio (Aardman, Laika) — physical errand heavier.

Khác với Production Assistant (similar role at film/TV) — Runner more specific to studio context. Khác với Production Coordinator (entry-mid management) — Runner pre-Coordinator. Khác với Intern (school-affiliated, time-limited) — Runner full-time job. Khác với Administrative Assistant (office admin focus) — Runner production-side. Hợp tác chặt với: Production Coordinator + Manager, Department Supervisor, Artist team, Studio operations, Executive + reception.

03Runner hoạt hình cần giỏi gì?

Work ethic + reliable

Show up on time. Complete task. Never complain about small task. Reliability foundation reputation. Best Runner make life easy cho team — get promoted fast.

Communication + courteous

Friendly + professional. Quick respond email. Hello everyone. Open + curious. Never act entitled.

Organize + detail-oriented

Multi-task. Track many small thing. Tool: Notion, Apple Notes, Google Keep. Calendar + list discipline. Catch detail others miss.

Animation industry passion

Genuine love + knowledge. Watch animation widely. Hiểu studio history + key film. Conversation animator + creator easy when passion shared.

Networking + relationship

Authentic relationship build. Long-term thinking. Help others — they help back. Read: "How to Win Friends and Influence People" (Carnegie), "Never Eat Alone" (Ferrazzi).

Eager learn + initiative

Ask question. Volunteer for extra. Find way add value beyond task assigned. Initiative most valuable trait for promotion.
