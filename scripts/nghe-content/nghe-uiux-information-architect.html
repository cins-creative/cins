Khi Amazon hiển thị product trong taxonomy Books → Fiction → Mystery → Cozy Mystery; khi Wikipedia có sitemap cross-reference 6 triệu article; khi VPBank app cho phép tìm Loan → Personal Loan → Quick Loan trong 2 tap — tất cả đều có Information Architect đã design cấu trúc thông tin.

Information Architecture (IA) là "đường ống nước" của UX — bạn không thấy, nhưng nếu đường ống lệch, mọi thứ trên trên cùng vỡ. IA là foundation invisible cho product — không có IA tốt, UI không thể tốt.

01Information Architect là ai?

Information Architect (Kiến trúc sư thông tin) là chuyên gia thiết kế cấu trúc + tổ chức thông tin trong digital product. Phân loại theo product type: (1) E-commerce IA — Amazon, Tiki, Shopee — product hierarchy + faceted search; (2) Content site IA — Wikipedia, news site, blog — taxonomy + tag; (3) SaaS / Enterprise IA — Salesforce, Slack, Notion — feature navigation + permission structure; (4) Mobile App IA — bottom tab, drawer, modal hierarchy; (5) Intranet / Knowledge base IA — internal company tool. Senior IA often work cross multiple product type.

Khác với UX Designer general (flow, interaction, research) — IA focus structure specifically. Khác với UI Designer (visual layer) — IA structural layer beneath. Khác với Content Strategist (what content) — IA how content organize. Khác với Data Architect (database structure) — IA user-facing structure. Hợp tác chặt với: UX Designer, UX Researcher, Content Strategist, Product Manager, Engineer, SEO Specialist.

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

IA work early trong project — define structure trước UI design begin. Báo cáo cho UX Director / Head of Design / Product Lead. Phối hợp với UX Researcher (insight → IA), UX Designer (IA → wireframe), Content team, Engineer (URL structure + database fit). Workflow: discovery + research (2–4 tuần) → IA draft (2–4 tuần) → user test (1–2 tuần) → iterate → handoff. Project: small redesign (4–8 tuần), full IA overhaul (3–9 tháng), enterprise IA (1–2 năm).

02Công việc của một Information Architect

1. Audit + research

For redesign: content audit — inventory existing content (URL, page, file). Hundreds-thousands page. Categorize, identify duplicate, dead content. User research — talk user, observe behavior, analyze analytic (search query, navigation path, drop-off). Identify mental model: how user expect content organized?

  • Content audit
  • User research
  • Mental model

2. Taxonomy + labeling

Build taxonomy — hierarchical categorization. Choose label wisely: clear, no jargon, match user mental model. Decide polyhierarchy (content fit multiple category) vs single hierarchy. Build controlled vocabulary for tag. Avoid label problem: ambiguous ("Resources"), overlapping ("News" vs "Articles" vs "Updates"), inconsistent capitalization.

  • Label clear, user-language
  • Controlled vocabulary

3. Sitemap + navigation design

Build sitemap: visual map of all page + relationship. Design navigation system: global nav (top bar — major section), local nav (sub-section), utility nav (login, search, language), breadcrumb. For mobile: bottom tab vs drawer vs hub-and-spoke. Cross-link strategy: related content, "you might also like".

4. Test + iterate + handoff

Validate với user: card sort (user group cards into category — informs taxonomy), tree test (user find item in proposed taxonomy — measure findability), first-click test (where do user click first to accomplish task?). Tool: Optimal Workshop, UserTesting. Iterate. Handoff sitemap + IA documentation tới UX Designer for wireframe phase.

03Information Architect cần giỏi gì?

📋 Systematic + structured thinking
✨ Taxonomy + library science
💬 User research + interview
🛠️ Tool: Optimal Workshop, Figma
⚡ Communication + diagramming
🎨 UX + product literacy
Systematic + structured thinking

Core. Think in hierarchy, relationship, taxonomy. Comfortable với abstract structure. Library science background bonus. Read: "Information Architecture for the World Wide Web" (Rosenfeld & Morville) — bible; "How to Make Sense of Any Mess" (Abby Covert).

Taxonomy + library science

Library science fundamental: cataloging, controlled vocabulary, thesaurus, faceted classification. Study: Dewey Decimal System, Library of Congress Classification. Modern app: SKOS (Simple Knowledge Organization System), schema.org. Read: "The Accidental Taxonomist" (Heather Hedden).

User research + interview

IA decision must ground user reality, not assumption. Comfort interview + observe. Run card sort + tree test session. Analyze qualitative data. Combine với analytic quantitative. Read: "Just Enough Research" (Erika Hall).

Tool: Optimal Workshop, Figma

IA tool: Optimal Workshop (card sort + tree test + first-click), UsabilityHub, UserTesting. Diagram: Figma + FigJam, Lucidchart, OmniGraffle (Mac classic), Whimsical. Spreadsheet: Excel/Google Sheets for content inventory.

Communication + diagramming

IA output = diagram + documentation. Must communicate clearly across stakeholder (designer, engineer, content, exec). Sitemap readable. Persuasive writing in spec. Present clearly in meeting.

UX + product literacy

IA exist trong UX context. Hiểu broader UX (user journey, persona, jobs-to-be-done). Hiểu product side (business goal, feature trade-off, technical constraint). Bridge role.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Information Architect?

1
Học UX / HCI / Library Science 3–5 năm

Background phù hợp: UX/HCI, Library & Information Science, Cognitive Science, Anthropology. VN: RMIT Digital Design, FPT University Multimedia. Quốc tế: CMU HCI, UC Berkeley iSchool, UMich School of Information, Pratt Information Experience Design. Plus: relevant book (Rosenfeld & Morville, Abby Covert).

2
UX / UI Designer with IA interest

Most IA start as UX/UI Designer. Specialize qua year. Volunteer for IA work on project — content audit, sitemap, taxonomy. Take IA-specific course (Nielsen Norman Group certification).

3
Junior / Mid IA role

3–5 năm experience — formal IA role. VN: large e-commerce + tech (Tiki, Shopee, Lazada, VNG, Zalo) — have IA team. International: Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Salesforce. Tasks: smaller portion of larger IA work.

4
Senior IA

5–8 năm — Senior. Lead full IA project. Define methodology. Mentor junior. Present to exec.

5
Lead / Principal IA

8–12 năm — Principal IA / IA Lead. Cross-product IA strategy. Influence engineering decision (database, URL structure). Industry expert.

6
Sự nghiệp Information Architect

Long-term path: (1) Principal IA tại major tech (Amazon, Google content team); (2) UX Director / Head of UX — IA strength foundation; (3) Independent consultant — IA niche valuable cho enterprise client; (4) Content Strategist / Knowledge Management lead; (5) Educator + author (Abby Covert, Peter Morville). Lương: VN IA mid 25–55M/tháng; senior 55M–120M; lead 120M-200M+. International: $85K-$130K mid; $130K-$200K senior; Principal $200K-$300K. Niche specialty với consistent demand từ enterprise + content-heavy product.
