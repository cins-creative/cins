Khi crew Avatar 2 quay tại New Zealand suốt 4 năm với ngân sách $400M, ai đảm bảo từng người được trả lương, từng chiếc trực thăng đến đúng giờ, từng catering đầy đủ, từng location permit hợp pháp? Đó là Line Producer.

Line Producer là người ground-level chuyển ý tưởng phim thành reality on set. Khác Executive Producer (financing, deal), Line Producer là "operations chief of staff" — họ live + breath ngân sách + lịch quay + crew + logistics suốt production. Không có Line Producer giỏi, phim collapse.

01Line Producer là ai?

Line Producer là Producer chịu trách nhiệm về day-to-day production logistics + ngân sách của phim. Phân loại theo project: (1) Feature film Line Producer — long-form, multi-month shoot, large crew; (2) TV series Line Producer — episodic, ongoing season; (3) Commercial / Advertising Line Producer — short-form, tight schedule, high-end cost; (4) Music video Line Producer — short-form artistic; (5) Documentary Line Producer — variable scope. Phân loại theo scale: Low-budget indie ($200K-$5M), Mid-tier ($5M-$50M), Studio film ($50M+).

Khác với Executive Producer (financing, top-level deal) — Line Producer ground-level execution. Khác với Producer general (creative + business) — Line Producer specifically logistics + budget. Khác với Production Manager (under Line Producer, day-to-day department) — Line Producer higher authority. Khác với UPM (Unit Production Manager) — overlap, sometimes same role with different title. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, Producer, UPM, Production Designer, Department Heads (DP, AD, Costume...), Studio Executive, Completion Bond company.

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Line Producer join trong pre-production (8-16 tuần before shoot), stay through production (shoot 4-20 tuần), partial post-production (wrap admin). Báo cáo Producer / Executive Producer / Studio. Authority over: UPM, Production Coordinator, Accountant, Department Heads (budget side). Workflow: receive script + ngân sách → break down → schedule + budget → hire crew → manage shoot → wrap. Project: indie film (4-9 tháng total commitment), studio film (12-24 tháng), TV season (6-12 tháng). Fee: % của ngân sách hoặc weekly flat (VN 15-40M/tuần mid, 40-100M/tuần senior).

02Công việc của một Line Producer

1. Pre-production — script breakdown + budget

Receive script. Break down: scene (count, location, day/night, INT/EXT), cast (lead + supporting + extras + stunt), crew need (number per department), equipment (camera, lighting, grip), location (cost, permit), special effect, VFX shot. Build shooting schedule. Build budget — 100+ line item từ above-the-line (cast, director, writer fee) tới below-the-line (crew, equipment, post, contingency).

  • Script breakdown
  • Shooting schedule
  • Budget chi tiết

2. Hire crew + vendor + negotiate

With Director, hire department heads (DP, Production Designer, Costume, Sound, Editor). Heads hire their crew under Line Producer ngân sách + approval. Negotiate rate for each. Hire vendor — equipment rental house, post house, catering, transport, insurance. Establish vendor contract. Manage petty cash + payroll.

3. Production — manage shoot day-to-day

On shooting day, Line Producer present (sometimes in production office, sometimes on set). Monitor budget actual vs plan daily. Approve spending. Solve crisis — actor sick (re-schedule), location cancel (find alternative), weather day (insurance claim, swap interior scene), equipment break. Manage overtime + penalty. Daily production report + cost report. Communicate up to producer + studio.

03Line Producer cần giỏi gì?

💰 Budgeting + financial control
📋 Production logistics + scheduling
⚡ Crisis management + problem solving
💬 Communication + negotiation
🎬 Film knowledge + craft understanding
🛠️ Tool: Movie Magic Scheduling, Budgeting, Excel
Budgeting + financial control

Core. Detailed knowledge of 100+ line item. Hiểu union rate, equipment cost, post cost. Track expense actual vs forecast daily. Foresee cost overrun + take action. Read: "The Hollywood Standard", "Movie Money Confidential".

Production logistics + scheduling

Schedule shoot day = puzzle. Optimize: minimize moving company, group location, work around actor availability + weather. Tools: Movie Magic Scheduling (MMS), StudioBinder, Yamdu. Hiểu day-out-of-days, strip board.

Crisis management + problem solving

Production luôn có crisis. Calm under pressure. Quick decision. Creative alternative thinking. Network for resource (last-minute equipment, replacement crew, emergency location).

Communication + negotiation

Talk với artist (sensitive ego), crew (union rule), vendor (contract), studio (budget pressure). Negotiate rate constantly. Diplomat. Strong written communication for production report.

Film knowledge + craft understanding

Hiểu craft of each department — không cần do technical work, nhưng phải understand what they need + why. Realistic estimate. Spot bullshit budget request. Industry experience essential.

Tool: Movie Magic Scheduling, Budgeting, Excel

Movie Magic Scheduling + Movie Magic Budgeting — industry standard. Excel + Google Sheets for custom calc. Slack, Asana, Frame.io for collaboration. QuickBooks for accounting.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Line Producer?

1
Học Film Production / Business / Finance 3–4 năm

Background: Film Production / Film Business / Business Administration. VN: ĐH Sân khấu Điện ảnh, ĐH Văn Lang Truyền thông Đa phương tiện, Hoa Sen Sản xuất phim. Quốc tế: USC Peter Stark Producing, UCLA Production, AFI Producing, NYU Tisch Producing. Plus: PGA (Producers Guild) seminar, line producer workshop.

2
Entry: Production Assistant (PA) 2–4 năm

Start: PA (production assistant) — coffee, paper, logistics support. Production Office Coordinator (POC). Production Secretary. Learn how production office work, who do what. VN: production company BHD, CJ HK Entertainment, Galaxy Studios, Lotte Cinema Production. Indie: Saigon Cinema Co., East Captain.

3
Production Coordinator → Production Manager 3–6 năm

Promote to Production Coordinator (manage paperwork, communication, scheduling). Then Production Manager / UPM (manage department, budget oversight). This is direct Line Producer prep path.

4
First Line Producer credit — indie / commercial

First Line Producer credit thường nhỏ — indie film, commercial, music video. Build reputation. Strong recommendation từ director + studio.

5
Mid-tier feature / TV series Line Producer

5-10 năm Line Producer experience. Take on mid-tier ngân sách film, TV series, premium commercial.

6
Sự nghiệp Line Producer

Long-term path: (1) Studio film Line Producer — large ngân sách Hollywood studio film; (2) Producer — promote to full Producer (creative + business + financing); (3) Studio executive — production company VP, head of production; (4) Independent producing partner; (5) International co-production specialist — VN attractive for international co-production (tax incentive); (6) Production company founder. Lương: VN Line Producer mid 25-60M/tháng (project rate); senior 60M-150M+/tháng. International: $5,000-$12,000/tuần mid-tier; $12,000-$25,000/tuần studio film; top Line Producer $25K-$40K+/tuần. Demand luôn cao — every shoot need Line Producer. Solid career với clear progression.
