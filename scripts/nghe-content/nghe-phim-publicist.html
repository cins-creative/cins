Media relationship + network

Built over years. Reporter, critic, editor, podcast host, social influencer relationship. Trust + reciprocity. Read book: "Hollywood Animal" (Eszterhas), follow trade press obsessively.

Storytelling + narrative

Find compelling angle in every project. Pitch story to press effectively. Craft narrative around film + talent. Write tight + engaging copy. Read: "Made to Stick" (Heath).

Event management

Premiere, festival, press junket. Logistics complex. Vendor coordinate. Crisis on-the-fly. Detail-oriented + composed under pressure.

Film industry deep knowledge

Know every player. Distribution model. Awards calendar + politics. Trade press dynamic. Film history + culture. Festival politics. Read: Variety, Hollywood Reporter, Deadline, IndieWire daily.

Crisis manage

Scandal contain. Bad review counter. Talent controversy manage. Cool under fire. Strategic + tactical thinking simultaneous. Read: "The Art of War" (Tzu), study crisis case (Will Smith Oscar slap, etc.).

International + cultural

Film global market. Different region different press culture. Multi-language English + local. Travel-ready (festival circuit). Cultural sensitivity for international talent.
