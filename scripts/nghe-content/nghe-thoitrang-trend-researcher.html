Social media monitor: TikTok, Instagram, Pinterest trend signal. Subculture explore: indie aesthetic, niche community (cottagecore, dark academia, gorpcore, balletcore). Art + film + music absorb: cross-cultural reference. Trade show: Première Vision, Heimtextil — material direction.

Observation + cultural eye

Notice subtle signal others miss. Curious + observational. Constantly absorb culture (film, art, music, news). Read: "Where Good Ideas Come From" (Johnson), "The Cool Hunter" (PSFK).

Pattern analysis + synthesis

Connect disparate signal. Distill noise to insight. Hypothesis test. Read: "Trend-Driven Innovation" (Mason), "The Trend Forecaster's Handbook" (Raymond).

Data + analytics literacy

Social listening tool (Brandwatch, Talkwalker). Trend search analytics (Google Trends, Trendalytics). Sales data analysis. Excel + Tableau + Power BI. Read: "Storytelling with Data" (Knaflic).

Writing + storytelling

Comprehensive report writing. Compelling narrative. Editor-quality prose. Read: "Made to Stick" (Heath), "On Writing Well" (Zinsser).

Global + cross-cultural

Travel + observe multiple market — Japan, Korea, Europe, US, emerging market. Multi-language helpful (English mandatory, French + Japanese + Korean valuable).

Communication + present

Keynote present. Workshop facilitate. Convince via data + visual. Read: "Resonate" (Duarte), "Presentation Zen" (Reynolds).
