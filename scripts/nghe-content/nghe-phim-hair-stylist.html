01Hair Stylist phim là ai?

Hair Stylist phim là chuyên gia thiết kế + tạo + maintain tóc cho diễn viên trong phim, TV, MV, fashion shoot. Hierarchy: (1) Hair Department Head — trưởng bộ phận, work với Director + Costume Designer; (2) Key Hair — daily lead on set; (3) Hair Stylist — work specific cast; (4) Hair Assistant. Phân loại theo project: Contemporary (normal hair), Period drama (era-accurate: 1920s flapper, 1950s pompadour, Victorian), Fantasy / Sci-fi (creative — Game of Thrones, Star Wars), Horror / Special FX (gore, prosthetic merge).

Khác với Salon Hair Stylist (commercial, daily client) — film stylist creative storytelling. Khác với Makeup Artist (focus face) — often crossover but Hair has own dept. Khác với Wigmaker (build wig from scratch — separate trade) — Hair Stylist apply + style wig. Hợp tác chặt với: Director, Costume Designer (period match), Makeup Artist (face + hair holistic), Production Designer, Actor.

  • Design hair concept theo character + era
  • Apply wig, extension, color
  • Daily continuity check
  • On-set maintenance + retouch

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Hair Department join trong pre-production (4–8 tuần trước shoot for feature, 1–2 tuần TV). Báo cáo cho Director / Producer; coordinate với Costume + Makeup. Pre-production: research, design, wig order/build (lead time 4–12 tuần custom wig). Production: morning prep (1–2 hours each actor), on-set retouch (constant), wrap clean. Daily call time 4-5am for actor có heavy hair. Feature 40–80 days; TV 7–10 days; commercial 1–5 days. Team: Hair Head + 2–8 stylist depending cast size.

03Hair Stylist phim cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Hair technical mastery
📋 Period + era knowledge
🛠️ Wig handling + maintenance
⚡ Continuity attention to detail
💬 Actor rapport + diplomacy
✨ Stamina + 14-hour day endurance
Hair technical mastery

Foundation. Hair school / barber school certification. Master: cut (precision, layered, undercut, blunt), color (highlight, balayage, dye, bleach), style (blowout, curl, perm, braid, updo), extension application, wig. 5–10 năm salon experience trước phim ideal.

Continue education: workshop, masterclass với top stylist (Vidal Sassoon Academy).

Period + era knowledge

Period drama — accuracy critical. Hair stylist must know: Ancient Egypt (kohl + braid), Greek/Roman, Medieval, Tudor, Victorian, 1920s flapper, 1950s, 1960s mod, 1970s, etc. Resource: "Hair Style History" book, museum visit, period painting study. VN period: 1960s áo dài Sài Gòn hair, 1980s perm.

Wig handling + maintenance

Wig = expensive ($1K-$10K+). Care: storage on canvas head, soft brush, gentle wash, restyle constantly. Apply skill: lace front (invisible hairline), perfect placement, secure tape/glue. Workshop với wig maker for deep knowledge.

Continuity attention to detail

Scene shot không order (Day 1 of story shot Day 50 production). Hair must match. Constant photo reference, detailed note. 1 hair flyaway different = editor disaster. Tablet ready, photo organized by scene + take.

Actor rapport + diplomacy

Actor trust stylist trong personal way. Build rapport. Discuss preference, listen concern (haircut anxiety, color worry). Diplomatic when actor request differ from director vision. Hours với actor in trailer build closeness — confidentiality important.

Stamina + 14-hour day endurance

Film day brutal. 4am call to 8pm wrap. On feet always (standing during retouch + watch). Lift heavy bag, gear. Mental focus — last actor on set still need fresh styling. Stay healthy, hydrate, sleep.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Hair Stylist phim?

1
Học hair / cosmetology + certify 1–2 năm

Background: Cosmetology school / Hair school. VN: Vidal Sassoon VN academy, L'Oréal Professional academy, Tony & Guy Vietnam, Aveda. Quốc tế: Vidal Sassoon Academy London, Bumble & Bumble University NY. License/certification required (varies country).

2
Salon work 5–8 năm

Build foundation. Salon allow practice all technique on hundreds head per năm. Specialize: editorial, bridal, color, cutting. VN top salon: Tony & Guy Saigon, Vidal Sassoon, Hair Republic, Anh Tài Salon, NHA Studio HN.

3
Build editorial / fashion portfolio

Step into editorial: collaborate với photographer + stylist on fashion shoot. Build portfolio editorial work. Indie short film + MV — entry into film world. VN: pitch HBT Productions, RedBoa, indie video team.

4
Film hair assistant / 2nd stylist

2–4 năm hair assistant on indie + commercial set. Learn film rhythm (extreme early call time, continuity, on-set politics). Network with Hair Department Head — get on bigger project.

5
Key Hair / Department Head

5–10 năm film — promote Key Hair then Department Head. Lead crew on full feature/series. Specialty develop: period drama specialist (Marie Antoinette type), action/stunt hair, music star tour stylist.

6
Sự nghiệp Hair Stylist phim

Long-term path: (1) Top Department Head với recurring star/director (Cate Blanchett's stylist, Beyoncé tour stylist); (2) Celebrity personal stylist — high-pay private engagement; (3) Own brand / salon with film stylist credentials; (4) Education + masterclass; (5) Crossover fashion editorial, MV, ad campaign. Lương: VN Hair Stylist 15–40M/tháng project-based; Key Hair 40–80M; Department Head 80–200M+/project; celebrity stylist 100M-500M+/event. International Hollywood union: $1K-$3K/day; Department Head $3K-$5K/day; Oscar-nominated Hair $500K-$1M+/năm. Niche but consistent demand.
