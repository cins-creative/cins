Khi feed Instagram của Highlands Coffee có visual consistency immediately recognizable, khi TikTok Vinamilk có template stylish lan tỏa, khi LinkedIn của Tiki có infographic professional thu hút engagement — đó là tác phẩm của Social Media Designer.

Social Media Designer là chuyên gia thiết kế visual content cho social platform — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube. Static post + story + reel + thumbnail. Role hot tại agency + brand + creator economy. Sản xuất volume cao, fast turnaround, platform-specific format mastery.

01Social Media Designer là ai?

Social Media Designer là chuyên gia design social content. Phân loại theo employer: (1) Brand in-house — Vinamilk, Highlands, Vingroup, FPT, Shopee social team; (2) Agency — social media agency, full-service agency social arm; (3) Creator / influencer team; (4) Media + publisher — VnExpress, Vietcetera, ZIO; (5) Freelance. Phân loại theo platform focus: (1) Instagram specialist — feed, story, reel; (2) TikTok specialist — vertical video, trend; (3) LinkedIn specialist — B2B professional; (4) YouTube specialist — thumbnail, channel art; (5) Cross-platform. Phân loại theo content type: (1) Static post; (2) Motion graphic + reel; (3) Infographic + data viz; (4) Meme + cultural.

Khác với Graphic Designer general — Social Media Designer platform-specific (Instagram aspect ratio vs LinkedIn vs Pinterest), trend-driven, volume. Khác với Content Creator (idea + caption + execution) — Social Media Designer visual execution focus. Khác với Motion Designer (animation) — overlap (reel + TikTok need motion). Khác với Brand Designer (brand system) — Social Media Designer apply brand to social, not define. Hợp tác chặt với: Social Media Manager / Content Strategist, Copywriter, Photographer, Video Editor, Brand Designer, Community Manager, Influencer.

Figma + Photoshop

Figma increasingly social design (component, prototype). Photoshop + Illustrator classic Adobe. Canva for non-design team + quick template (Social Designer should know). Procreate illustration. InDesign rarely.

After Effects + CapCut

After Effects for motion. Premiere Pro for video edit. CapCut (TikTok-native) for vertical short. InShot, Splice mobile editor. Motion design skill critical for reel + TikTok era.

Platform format mastery

Instagram: 1:1 feed, 9:16 story + reel, 4:5 portrait. TikTok: 9:16 vertical, 60s sweet spot. LinkedIn: 1.91:1 banner, 1200x628 post. YouTube thumbnail 1280x720. Pinterest 2:3 vertical. Each platform algorithm preference unique.

Trend + culture literacy

Daily scroll social + absorb trend. Meme literacy. Gen-Z / Gen-Alpha aesthetic awareness. Cultural sensitivity. Follow trend report (TikTok Creative Center, Instagram Trend report).

Speed + volume

Pump out 10-30 asset/tuần. Template system + Figma component. Fast iteration. Comfortable với constant feedback. No perfectionism (good enough + ship fast).

Analytics + insight

Read Instagram Insights, TikTok Analytics, Facebook Insights. Hiểu metric (reach, impression, engagement rate, save, share). Apply learning to design choice.
