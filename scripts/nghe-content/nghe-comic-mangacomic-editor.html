01Manga/Comic Editor là ai?

Manga/Comic Editor là chuyên gia làm việc với mangaka / comic writer/artist xuyên suốt quá trình sáng tác. Phân loại theo industry: (1) Manga Editor (Nhật Bản) — Shueisha (One Piece, Naruto), Kodansha (Attack on Titan), Shogakukan — work weekly với mangaka; (2) Western Comic Editor — Marvel, DC, Image, IDW, Dark Horse — work monthly với writer + artist team; (3) Graphic Novel Editor — long-form one-shot (First Second, Drawn & Quarterly); (4) Webcomic Editor — Webtoon (Naver, Kakao), Tapas — Korean industry leader; (5) VN Comic Editor — Phan Thị, Kim Đồng, Comicola, NXB Trẻ.

Khác với Book Editor general (novel, non-fiction) — Comic Editor visual + sequential. Khác với Writer / Mangaka — Editor not main creator, partner. Khác với Letterer (text in comic) — Editor manage whole production. Hợp tác chặt với: Mangaka / Writer / Artist, Editor-in-Chief, Marketing, Sales, Translator (cho international license), Licensee, Lettering team, Print + digital production.

  • Partner với mangaka / writer develop story
  • Manage schedule + deadline
  • Coordinate publication + marketing
  • Scout + develop new talent

03Manga/Comic Editor cần giỏi gì?

📚 Story + narrative literacy
🎨 Visual + comic literacy
💬 Creator relationship + communication
📋 Project + production management
📈 Market sense + business
⚡ Stamina + work ethic
Story + narrative literacy

Foundation. Read enormously — manga (every genre: shonen, shojo, seinen, josei), Western comic, novel, screenplay. Hiểu narrative structure (3 act, Kishōtenketsu Japanese), character arc, pacing. Read: "Story" (Robert McKee), "Save the Cat", "Manga in Theory and Practice" (Hirohiko Araki).

Visual + comic literacy

Hiểu visual storytelling — panel composition, page layout, reading flow. Hiểu craft của penciller, inker, letterer enough to evaluate work. Read: "Understanding Comics" (Scott McCloud), "Making Comics" (McCloud), "Manga! Manga!" (Frederik Schodt).

Creator relationship + communication

Creator = artist với strong emotion. Editor = close + sometimes confrontational partner. Build trust + respect. Communicate hard note constructively. Defend creator vision against marketing pressure when right. Push when needed.

Project + production management

Manage multiple series. Tool: spreadsheet, Notion, Asana. Track deadline. Coordinate cross-team (letterer, colorist, marketing). Reliability + organization essential.

Market sense + business

Understand market: what genre selling, who target audience, what tone resonating. Manga editor pay attention reader survey (Shueisha system shapes industry). Business literacy: licensing, royalty, contract.

Stamina + work ethic

Manga editor at Shueisha = legendary work hour (60-80 hr/week, 7 day). Mangaka working through night → editor available. Western comic less intense but still demanding. Passion for craft sustain.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Manga/Comic Editor?

1
Học Văn / Editing / Comic Studies 3–4 năm

Background: Văn học / Báo chí / Editing / Comics Studies / Japanese Studies. VN: ĐH KHXH&NV Văn học / Báo chí, ĐH Sư phạm Nhật ngữ. Quốc tế: UCLA + UCI Comics Studies, UPenn Comics Studies, SCAD Sequential Art, Kyoto Seika Manga. Plus: read 1000s of manga + comic. Learn Japanese essential cho Japanese editor track.

2
Entry: Editorial Assistant / Junior Editor

VN publisher: NXB Kim Đồng, NXB Trẻ, Phan Thị, Comicola, NXB Hồng Đức. Tasks: license review, translation coordinate, proofread, fan event. Some VN editor work international scout (cho international platform like Tapas, Webtoon, Manta).

3
Mid Editor — handle own series

3-6 năm — manage 3-5 series. Develop new talent. Get story note responsibility. International publisher: Shueisha, Kodansha, Shogakukan (Japan), Marvel, DC, Image, IDW, BOOM!, Dark Horse (US), Naver Webtoon, Kakao Page (Korea).

4
Senior Editor

6-12 năm — Senior with name attached to successful series. Mentor junior. Influence new series greenlight.

5
Editor-in-Chief / Department Head

12-20 năm — Editor-in-Chief at magazine (Weekly Shonen Jump editor-in-chief = legendary role). Set editorial direction. Greenlight series. Manage editor team.

6
Sự nghiệp Manga/Comic Editor

Long-term path: (1) Editor-in-Chief / Publisher — top role at major publisher; (2) Founder publishing studio — independent publishing; (3) Agent / Manager for creator; (4) Cross-media producer — adapt comic to anime / film (Aniplex, Toho production); (5) Webcomic platform exec — Naver, Tapas senior role; (6) Academic + author — comic studies. Lương: VN Comic Editor junior 10-22M/tháng; mid 22-50M; senior 50M-120M; EIC 120M-300M+. International: $50K-$80K mid (US); $80K-$150K senior; EIC $150K-$300K. Japanese manga editor at Shueisha tier-1: ¥7-15M/năm mid; ¥15-30M senior; EIC ¥30M+. Niche prestigious craft. VN growing với webcomic + manhua / manga import market booming.
