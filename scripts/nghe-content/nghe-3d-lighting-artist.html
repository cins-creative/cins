033D Lighting Artist cần giỏi gì?

📷 Studio photography knowledge
🎨 Color + light theory
🛠️ Tool: 3ds Max, Maya, Blender, V-Ray, Corona, Arnold, Octane, Lumion
⚡ Render optimization + farm
✨ Material + reflection understanding
💬 Client communication
Studio photography knowledge

Foundation. Study product photography: light tent, softbox, snoot, beauty dish, gobo. Hiểu setup: rim light, kicker, hair light, ground light. Subscribe magazine: Communication Arts, Lürzer's Archive. YouTube: Karl Taylor, Dustin Dolby (Workphlo).

Color + light theory

Color temperature (Kelvin), color theory, light behavior (inverse square law, falloff, bounce). Read: "Color and Light" (James Gurney), "Light Science and Magic" (Hunter et al).

Tool: 3ds Max, Maya, Blender, V-Ray, Corona, Arnold, Octane, Lumion

3ds Max + V-Ray — archviz + product standard VN. Maya + Arnold — premium product viz. Blender + Cycles — indie + growing. Cinema 4D + Octane — advertising favorite. Corona Renderer — archviz fast iteration. Lumion / Enscape / Twinmotion — real-time archviz. KeyShot — quick product viz industry standard. Unreal Engine 5 — emerging hi-end real-time.

Render optimization + farm

Hi-res render = hours. Optimize sample count, AA, GI bounce. Use render farm (RebusFarm, Ranch Computing, local farm). Plan render schedule. Manage memory (large scene = lot of VRAM/RAM).

Material + reflection understanding

Light interact với material — without good material, light fall flat. Hiểu PBR (physically based rendering), Fresnel, anisotropic, SSS (skin, jade, marble). Reflection critical for product viz — chrome, metal, glass, polished wood. Work closely với texturing artist.

Client communication

Client often non-technical. Listen carefully. Translate vague request ("more wow") into technical action. Manage expectation about render time. Document choice rationale.
