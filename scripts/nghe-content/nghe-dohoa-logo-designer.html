03Logo Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Conceptual thinking + visual abstraction
📋 Typography mastery
🛠️ Tool: Illustrator, Procreate sketching
📐 Geometry + form sensitivity
💬 Client communication + presentation
📚 Brand strategy + business literacy
Conceptual thinking + visual abstraction

Core. Compress brand idea into single mark. Think analogy, metaphor, hidden meaning. Famous case: FedEx hidden arrow giữa E + x (forward motion), Amazon arrow A → Z (all product, smile face). Study: "Logo Modernism" (Jens Müller), "Logo Design Love" (David Airey).

Typography mastery

Half of logo work = typography. Wordmark, lettermark heavy on type. Hiểu type anatomy, font family, custom letter modification. Reference: "Thinking with Type" (Lupton), "Logotype" (Michael Evamy).

Tool: Illustrator, Procreate sketching

Adobe Illustrator — industry standard (vector). Procreate / iPad for sketch + refine. Figma — for brand system + handoff. Affinity Designer — alternative. InDesign for brand guideline document. Pen + sketchbook always.

Geometry + form sensitivity

Eye for line weight, curve smoothness, optical balance, golden ratio. Logo must work at 16x16px favicon and 30 meter signage. Master grid + construction. Study geometry of master: Apple, Mastercard, Pepsi grid construction.

Client communication + presentation

Logo design = high emotional stake for client. Manage expectation. Defend design rationale with story + data. Handle "make logo bigger" type feedback gracefully. Strong written + verbal communication. Read: "Logo Design Love" (David Airey) — chapters on client management.

Brand strategy + business literacy

Hiểu brand strategy fundamental — positioning, audience, competitor. Hiểu business model — startup vs enterprise need different logo style. Read: "Designing Brand Identity" (Alina Wheeler).
