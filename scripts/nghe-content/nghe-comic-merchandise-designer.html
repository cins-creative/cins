01Merchandise Designer là ai?

Merchandise Designer (Comic / Anime IP) là chuyên gia thiết kế sản phẩm merchandise sử dụng character + asset từ IP. Phân loại theo sản phẩm: (1) Apparel + Fashion — T-shirt, hoodie, bag (Uniqlo UT, BoxLunch); (2) Toy + Figure — action figure, statue, Funko Pop, Nendoroid; (3) Accessory + Stationery — keychain, badge, sticker, notebook; (4) Home + Lifestyle — mug, poster, blanket, home decor; (5) Premium Collectible — limited edition, hot toy, statue; (6) Food + Beverage tie-in — branded snack, cafe themed. Phân loại theo level: Mass market (high volume, low margin), Premium collectible (low volume, high margin), Limited edition (artist collab).

Khác với Comic Artist (create IP) — Merchandise Designer use existing IP. Khác với Product Designer general (ergonomic, function-focused) — Merch Designer IP-driven, fan-driven. Khác với Toy Designer general — Merch Designer specifically licensed IP product. Hợp tác chặt với: IP holder / Licensor (publisher, creator), Licensee manufacturer, Brand Manager, Retail buyer, Quality control, Marketing.

  • Thiết kế product với IP character
  • Coordinate licensor + manufacturer
  • Maintain brand integrity
  • Manage production + quality

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Merchandise Designer thường tại licensee manufacturer (Bandai, Funko, Hot Toys) hoặc IP holder side (publisher merchandising department) hoặc licensing agency. Báo cáo Product Manager / Brand Manager / Creative Director. Workflow: receive IP guideline → concept product → IP holder approval → prototype → sample production → quality test → mass production → retail launch. Project: single product (2-6 tháng), collection launch (6-12 tháng), tied to anime / film release.

02Công việc của một Merchandise Designer

1. Receive IP brief + license + plan

Receive IP material: character model sheet, color guide, brand bible, license restriction (what allowed: PG-rated only, no weapon for kid line). Study license territory (global / regional), exclusivity terms, royalty rate (typical 8-15%). Plan product range: what SKU launch, target customer, price point. Build concept with mood board + sketch.

  • IP guideline study
  • License terms
  • Product plan

2. Design + IP holder approval

Design product: apparel — print placement, color combo, fit; figure — pose, accessory, base; accessory — character icon, key art. Tool: Adobe Illustrator + Photoshop for 2D; ZBrush + Maya for figure sculpt. IP holder approval round: submit design — they review for character accuracy, brand fit, no off-model. Multiple iteration. Japanese IP (Shonen Jump, Toho) particularly strict — character must match exactly.

3. Prototype + sample + quality test

After design approved: build prototype. For apparel: tech pack to factory, sample garment review. For figure: 3D print first, then production mold made. Sample production: small batch, test material, paint, durability, safety (kids product must meet safety standard — non-toxic paint, no small part choking hazard). Quality issue address before mass production. Cost negotiation with factory.

03Merchandise Designer cần giỏi gì?

🎨 Product + graphic design
📚 IP + character study
🛠️ Tool: Illustrator, Photoshop, ZBrush, Maya
📋 Manufacturing + materials knowledge
📈 Market + retail awareness
💬 Licensor + factory communication
Product + graphic design

Foundation. Eye for product form + graphic application. Hiểu print design (separation, color limit cho screen printing), embroidery, sublimation. For 3D product: sculpt, paint, posing. Study existing merchandise: Bandai's S.H.Figuarts series, Hot Toys, Mafex.

IP + character study

Hardcore fan + nerd of IP. Know character minute detail: weapon, signature pose, color exact, secondary character. Watch all anime / read all manga of target series. Respect IP soul.

Tool: Illustrator, Photoshop, ZBrush, Maya

Adobe Illustrator + Photoshop — graphic design tool. ZBrush + Maya for figure sculpt. Marvelous Designer for apparel pattern. KeyShot / Octane for product render. InDesign for catalog + packaging.

Manufacturing + materials knowledge

Hiểu manufacturing constraint: injection molding (figure), screen print + digital print + sublimation + embroidery (apparel), die-cut + foil (sticker). Material: PVC, ABS, polystone, cotton, polyester, blends. Factory liaison.

Market + retail awareness

Study retail trend, fanbase preference, price point sensitivity. Follow: Crunchyroll Store, Sideshow, Hot Toys, Bandai shop, Box Lunch, Hot Topic. VN retail: Pomi Shop, Anime VN, MnB Comic Shop. Convention: Comic Con SDCC, AnimeJapan, VietCon.

Licensor + factory communication

Diplomat. Communicate creative vision to Japanese / US licensor (often demand). Negotiate with factory (often Chinese supplier). Patience + clarity. English required.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Merchandise Designer?

1
Học Product Design / Graphic / Industrial Design 3–4 năm

Background: Product Design / Industrial Design / Graphic Design / Toy Design. VN: ĐH Mỹ thuật Công nghiệp Industrial Design, FPT Arena, RMIT Industrial Design, Hutech Đồ họa. Quốc tế: Otis College Toy Design, Pratt Industrial Design, Art Center College of Design. Plus: anime + manga fan obsession from young.

2
Junior at licensee manufacturer / agency 2–4 năm

VN: Pomi Shop (retail + own brand), Anime VN merchandise. International: Bandai Tokyo, Banpresto, Good Smile Company, Funko, Hot Toys (HK), Hasbro, Bioworld (apparel). Some IP holder side: Disney Consumer Products, Marvel Brands, Pokemon Co.

3
Mid Merchandise Designer 4–7 năm

Own product line. Pitch to licensor. Manage cross-collection. Build relationship with retail buyer + manufacturer.

4
Senior Designer / Lead

7-12 năm — Lead product line. Define brand direction. Mentor junior. Pitch new IP partnership.

5
Brand / Product Manager / Director

12+ năm — Manager track. Strategic planning. P&L responsibility for product line. Negotiate license deal.

6
Sự nghiệp Merchandise Designer

Long-term path: (1) Brand / Product Director at licensee — Bandai, Funko, Hot Toys; (2) Consumer Products lead at IP holder — Disney, Marvel, Pokemon Co; (3) Founder own merch brand — independent (Pomi Shop model VN, Mondo USA); (4) Designer toy artist — collab limited edition (KAWS-style art toy boundary); (5) Licensing consultant + agent — broker IP-manufacturer deal; (6) Independent illustrator + merch shop — sell own design Etsy / DesignByHumans. Lương: VN Merchandise Designer mid 15-35M/tháng; senior 35M-80M+; Director 80M-200M+. International: $60K-$95K mid; $95K-$170K senior; Director $170K-$300K. Demand bùng nổ với anime + manga globalization + Pokemon-Squid Game-Demon Slayer level hit. Passion-driven + commercially scalable.
