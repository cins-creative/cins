Paint / Cleanup Artist là chuyên gia clean unwanted element trong footage VFX — wire, rig, blemish, mic shadow, set imperfection, unwanted person. Foundation invisible role — work tốt khi audience không notice. Critical bước trước compositing + further VFX layer.

01Paint / Cleanup Artist là ai?

Paint / Cleanup Artist (cũng gọi Roto-Paint Artist) là chuyên gia paint hand-frame để remove unwanted element + restore background plate. Phân loại theo task: (1) Wire + rig removal — fly wire stunt, rig hold actor; (2) Blemish + beauty cleanup — pimple, scar, wrinkle (actor) — overlap với beauty work; (3) Set imperfection — boom mic, light reflection, crew accidental in frame; (4) Set extension cleanup — remove element không thuộc time period; (5) Tattoo removal — character không có tattoo trong story nhưng actor có; (6) License plate / branding removal — legal clearance. Phân loại theo experience: Junior Roto-Paint (simple cleanup), Mid (complex), Senior Cleanup Lead.

Khác với Compositor (broader composite layer) — Paint focus pure removal + restoration. Khác với Roto Artist (matte extraction, mask creation) — Paint actually paint pixel; overlap heavy though, thường same person. Khác với Matte Painter (create new background painting) — Paint restore existing. Khác với Beauty Artist (cosmetic skin perfection) — Paint broader cleanup. Hợp tác chặt với: Roto Artist, Compositor, VFX Supervisor, Tracker, 3D Artist, Production Coordinator.

  • Remove wire, rig, unwanted element
  • Restore clean background plate
  • Track shot cho stable paint across frame
  • Maintain photoreal seamless

Vị trí trong quy trình sản xuất

Paint / Cleanup Artist work tại VFX studio — major (ILM, Weta, DNEG, Framestore, MPC) + mid-tier (Sparx*, Bad Clay) + indie. Báo cáo Roto-Paint Supervisor / Compositing Supervisor. Workflow per shot: receive plate + brief → analyze (1-2 giờ) → set up tracking + paint approach → paint frame-by-frame hoặc track-based (per shot 1 ngày - 1 tuần) → review + iterate → deliver clean plate cho Compositor / 3D team.

02Công việc của một Paint / Cleanup Artist

1. Plate analysis + approach planning

Receive plate + brief from supervisor. Analyze: what element remove, motion (handheld vs locked-off), lighting, occlusion (element passing in front), grain pattern. Plan approach: 2D paint stroke per frame (handheld + complex), tracked patch (locked-off plate, repeat motion), 3D projection (architectural set), clean plate generate from frame elsewhere.

  • Plate analyze
  • Approach decide
  • Setup project

3. Frame-by-frame paint

Complex shot need frame-by-frame paint. Tool: Nuke Paint node (industry standard), Silhouette, Mocha Pro. Use RotoPaint stroke, clone, healing, blend mode. Match grain + color + blur of original. Maintain across frame for stable result. Patience + meticulous work.

03Paint / Cleanup Artist cần giỏi gì?

Nuke + Silhouette + Mocha mastery

Nuke (Foundry) — industry standard, primary tool. Master RotoPaint, Tracker node, expression. Silhouette — paint + roto specialty (excellent for paint specifically). Mocha Pro — planar tracking + roto. Course: Foundry tutorial, FXPHD, SilhouetteFX tutorial.

Photoshop fluent

Photoshop skill help — clone + healing + blend mode similar concept. Hand-paint approach learn from Photoshop. Sometime use Photoshop for clean plate prep.

Tracking + matchmoving basic

Tracking fundamental for paint. Hiểu camera tracking, planar tracking, 3D tracking. Solve track problem yourself. Pass complex to dedicated Tracker artist.

Detail obsession + patience

Frame-by-frame work tedious. Spot single-pixel flicker. Detail obsessive. Patient. Persistent. Work invisible — only appreciate quality when fail. Mindset critical.

Cinematography + grain knowledge

Hiểu camera + lens + grain pattern. Different camera (Alexa, RED, Sony Venice) different grain. Lens distortion. ISO setting affect noise. Match perfect.

Production speed + organization

Per shot deadline tight. Build personal template + workflow. Organized node graph + version. Speed valuable competitive advantage.

04Làm cách nào để trở thành Paint / Cleanup Artist?

1
Học VFX / Digital Media 2–4 năm

Background: VFX / Visual Effects / Digital Media / Animation. VN: SKDA Hà Nội, FPT Arena VFX, Arena Multimedia, MAAC. Quốc tế: Gnomon School VFX, Lost Boys School VFX, Vancouver Film School, Think Tank Training Centre, SCAD VFX. Plus online: FXPHD, CGMA Roto Paint course, Nuke training.

2
Junior Roto-Paint Artist 2–4 năm

VN VFX studio entry-level common: Bad Clay Studio, Sparx*, Glassbox Animation, Tinker Studio, Cyclo Animation. International: ILM, Weta, DNEG, Framestore, MPC, Method, Cinesite, Pixomondo, RodeoFX — major hire many entry-level for paint work. Roto-Paint is common entry door to VFX career.

3
Mid Paint Artist 4–7 năm

Lead complex paint shot. Mentor junior. Specialize: stunt removal, beauty cleanup, set extension.

4
Senior Paint Artist / Lead 7–12 năm

Lead paint department on project. Train + mentor team. Establish workflow standard.

5
Roto-Paint Supervisor / Compositor transition 12+ năm

Supervise entire roto + paint pipeline. Hoặc transition to Compositor career — natural progression with paint foundation.

6
Sự nghiệp Paint / Cleanup Artist

Long-term path: (1) Compositor transition — most common path (broader role, higher pay); (2) Roto-Paint Supervisor — specialize as Lead; (3) Compositing Supervisor — overall composite leadership; (4) VFX Supervisor — career peak; (5) Independent freelance; (6) Educator. Lương: VN Paint Artist junior 10-22M/tháng; mid 22-45M; senior 45M-90M; Lead 90M-170M+. International: $50K-$80K mid; $80K-$140K senior; Lead $140K-$220K+. Paint career entry-level friendly + clear progression path. Many VFX career start here.
