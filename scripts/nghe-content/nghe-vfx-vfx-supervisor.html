01VFX Supervisor studio-side là ai?

VFX Supervisor studio-side là top creative + technical leader tại VFX vendor studio. Phân loại theo focus: (1) Show VFX Supervisor — lead specific show; (2) Department Supervisor — CG, Compositing, FX, Layout, Lighting (subset of show); (3) Senior VFX Supervisor — oversee multiple show; (4) Head of Show — production-facing senior lead. Phân loại theo career: (1) Senior Artist / TD 5-10 năm (path); (2) Department Supervisor 10-15 năm; (3) VFX Supervisor 15-20 năm; (4) Senior VFX Supervisor + Head of Show 20-25 năm; (5) Studio Creative Director 25+ năm. Phân loại theo background: (1) CG Supervisor — modeling, animation, FX background; (2) Compositing Supervisor — Nuke + 2D background; (3) FX Supervisor — Houdini, simulation; (4) Lighting / Lookdev Supervisor; (5) Generalist Supervisor — broad. Phân loại theo studio: (1) Tier 1 — ILM, Weta, DNEG, Framestore; (2) Tier 2 — MPC, Method, Rodeo FX, Cinesite; (3) Boutique; (4) Asian VFX hub — Bad Clay, Sparx*-Virtuos.

Hợp tác chặt với: Production-side VFX Supervisor (counterpart), Department Supervisor (CG, Comp, FX), VFX Producer + Coordinator, VFX Artist team, Director (occasional), Studio Creative Director + Head of Show.

VFX pipeline depth

Every department + interdependency. Read: "The VES Handbook of Visual Effects", "Digital Compositing for Film and Video" (Wright), "Production Pipeline Fundamentals for Film and Games" (Renee Dunlop).

Maya + Houdini + Nuke expert

Tool master across pipeline. Demonstrate at expert level. Script-friendly (Python). Pipeline tool literacy.

Visual + composition eye

Cinematography sense. Mood + tone. Color + lighting. Composition. Photoreal vs stylized judgment. Read: "Color and Light" (Gurney), "The Filmmaker's Eye" (Mercado).

Team leadership

Multi-department + multi-cultural team. Note give kindly. Motivate during crunch. Read: "Drive" (Pink), "Multipliers" (Wiseman).

Production-side liaison

Diplomatic. Translate creative note to actionable. Push back tactfully on scope creep. Long-term relationship build với production company.

Pressure + crunch resilience

VFX crunch chronic. Long hour ability. Composure under deadline. Health management.
