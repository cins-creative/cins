02Công việc của một Product Visualizer 3D

1. CAD import + modeling refinement

Receive CAD file from Industrial Designer (SolidWorks, Rhino, Inventor) hoặc build from scratch. Convert + clean: CAD NURBS → mesh, retopologize for render (subdivision-friendly). Add detail: micro bevel (no real edge perfectly sharp), screw, joint, small feature for photoreal. Tool: Maya, Cinema 4D, 3ds Max, Blender, Rhino.

  • CAD import + clean
  • Retopology
  • Detail refine

03Product Visualizer 3D cần giỏi gì?

🛠️ Modeling + topology
🎨 Texturing + PBR material
💡 Lighting + photography eye
⚙️ Render engine mastery
📷 Composition + camera
🛍️ Brand + product sense
Modeling + topology

Hard surface modeling primary. Master Maya, Cinema 4D, 3ds Max, Blender. Hiểu subdivision surface, NURBS, mesh topology. Quad-dominant. Edge support cho subdivision. CAD interop (SolidWorks, Rhino).

Texturing + PBR material

Substance Painter + Substance Designer industry standard. PBR workflow: base color, roughness, metallic, normal, height. Material library knowledge. Real-world reference. Read: "The PBR Guide" (Allegorithmic/Substance).

Lighting + photography eye

Foundation. Hiểu photography lighting: 3-point, softbox, ring light, naturalistic. Study product photography work — Apple, Vogue product shoot. Lighting = mood + brand. Study: Light Science & Magic (Hunter).

Render engine mastery

Octane (GPU, fast, popular cho product), Redshift (GPU, broader DCC support), Arnold (CPU, industry standard film), V-Ray (popular archviz + product), Cycles (Blender), Karma (Houdini). Optimize render time + quality balance.

Composition + camera

Product hero shot composition. Rule of third. Eye flow. Camera angle (hero high, dramatic low, eye level). Lens choice (50mm classic, macro for detail). Crop. Study product photography master.

Brand + product sense

Hiểu brand language. Apple minimal soft vs Rolex luxury hard. Each brand has signature look. Replicate / adapt cho brand consistency. Marketing language thinking.
